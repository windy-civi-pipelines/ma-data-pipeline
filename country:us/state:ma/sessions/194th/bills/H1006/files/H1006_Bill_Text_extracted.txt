HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2394 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1006
By Representative Meschino of Hull, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1006) of Joan
Meschino for an investigation by a special commission relative to incentive-based approaches to
increasing carbon removal and sequestration by natural and working lands. Environment and
Natural Resources.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to incentives for nature as a climate solution.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 There shall be a task force to investigate and make recommendations on new or expanded
2 policy for incentive-based approaches to increasing carbon removal and sequestration by natural
3 and working lands consistent with, and designed to meet, the enforceable natural and working
4 lands sublimit contained in section 3A(a) of chapter 21N of the General Laws.
5 The task force shall include the undersecretary of environment, who shall serve as chair,
6 the climate chief or a designee from the office of climate innovation and resilience, the
7 commissioner of conservation and recreation, and the commissioner of fish and game. The task
8 force shall include additional members appointed by the chair, which shall include
9 representatives of municipalities, statewide land conservation organizations, and land trusts, as
10 well as experts in the forest ecology and terrestrial carbon, wetlands carbon, conservation
11 finance, and environmental and climate mitigation economics.
1 of 2
12 The task force shall develop policy recommendations and technical considerations for
13 approaches and incentives to increase investment in natural and working lands carbon by public
14 and private entities. Such recommendations may include, but not be limited to, proposed
15 legislation, regulatory structures, and elements of the clean energy and climate plans as required
16 by chapter 21N of the General Laws.
17 The task force shall file its findings and recommendations with the clerks of the house of
18 representatives and the senate, the house and senate chairs of the joint committee on
19 telecommunications, utilities and energy, the house and senate chairs of the joint committee on
20 environment and natural resources, and the chairs of the house and senate committees on global
21 warming and climate change not later than December 31, 2026.
2 of 2
