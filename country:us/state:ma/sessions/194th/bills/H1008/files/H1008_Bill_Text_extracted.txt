HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 693 FILED ON: 1/11/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1008
By Representative Moakley of Falmouth and Senator Cyr, a joint petition (accompanied by bill,
House, No. 1008) of Thomas W. Moakley, Julian Cyr and Hadley Luddy for legislation to
authorize the establishment of the Manuel F. Correllus State Forest trust fund. Environment and
Natural Resources.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act creating the Manuel F. Correllus State Forest Trust Fund.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 92 of the General Laws are hereby amended by inserting after section 34D the
2 following section:-
3 “Section 34E: Notwithstanding any general or special law or administrative bulletin to
4 the contrary and pursuant to section 34, there is hereby established and set up on the books of the
5 commonwealth a separate fund, to be known as the Manuel F. Correllus State Forest Trust Fund,
6 which shall be used for the purposes of advancing recreational, educational and conservation
7 interests, including, but not limited to, the construction and maintenance of facilities and
8 infrastructure improvements for the area within the forest complex. The trust shall receive, hold
9 and expend all fees generated by permits, licenses, easements, and all other agreements not
10 currently being directed to the General Fund relating to the use of the forest complex land as
11 authorized by the commission. The department shall not make expenditures from this fund so as
12 to cause the fund to be deficient.”
