HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1272 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1064
By Representative Vieira of Falmouth, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1064) of
David T. Vieira relative to changing the name of the Inland Fisheries and Game Fund to the
George L. Darey inland fisheries and game fund. Environment and Natural Resources.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 918 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to a technical change to the George L. Darey Inland Fisheries and Game Fund.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 10G of chapter 21A of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2020
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out, in line 90, the words “Inland Fish” and
3 inserting in place thereof the following words:- Georgy L. Darey Inland Fisheries.
4 SECTION 2. Section 17 of chapter 58 of the General Laws, as so appearing, is hereby
5 amended by inserting after the word “the”, in line 9, the following words:- George L. Darey.
6 SECTION 3. Section 13 of chapter 64A of the General Laws, as so appearing, is hereby
7 amended by inserting after the word “the”, in line 12, the following words:- George L. Darey.
8 SECTION 4. Section 2A of chapter 131 of the General Laws, as so appearing, is hereby
9 amended by inserting after the word “the”, in line 1, the following words:- George L. Darey.
1 of 2
10 SECTION 5. Section 2B of said chapter 131, as so appearing, is hereby amended by
11 inserting after the word “the”, in line 5, the following words:- George L. Darey.
12 SECTION 6. Section 3 of said chapter 131, as so appearing, is hereby amended by
13 inserting after the word “the”, in line 7, the following words:- George L. Darey.
14 SECTION 7. Section 4 of said chapter 131, as so appearing, is hereby amended by
15 inserting after the word “the”, in line 190, the following words:- George L. Darey.
16 SECTION 8. Section 11 of said chapter 131, as so appearing, is hereby amended by
17 inserting after the word “the”, in line 81, the following words:- George L. Darey.
18 SECTION 9. Section 22A of said chapter 131, as so appearing, is hereby amended by
19 inserting after the word “the”, in line 14, the following words:- George L. Darey.
20 SECTION 10. Section 14 of chapter 101 of the Acts of 2003 is hereby repealed.
2 of 2
