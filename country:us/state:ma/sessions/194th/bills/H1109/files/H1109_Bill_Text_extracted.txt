HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1274 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1109
By Representative Chaisson of Foxborough, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1109)
of Michael S. Chaisson relative to motor vehicle underinsurance. Financial Services.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 947 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to relative to an underinsurance issue.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Paragraph 2 of Section 113L of Chapter 175 of the General Laws, as so appearing in the
2 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after the first sentence the following
3 sentence: -
4 The coverage described in this paragraph shall also apply when, by reason of payment of
5 judgment or settlement, the bond amount or policy limit of the tortfeasor has been reduced by the
6 payment of multiple claims arising from the same accident to an amount less than the policy
7 limit for uninsured motor vehicle coverage, and only to the extent that the uninsured motor
8 vehicle coverage limits exceed the amount paid to the insured pursuant to the exhausted bodily
9 injury liability bond or policy.
