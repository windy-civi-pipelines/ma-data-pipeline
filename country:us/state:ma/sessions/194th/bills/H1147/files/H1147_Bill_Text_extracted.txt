HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1103 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1147
By Representative Duffy of Holyoke, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1147) of
Patricia A. Duffy and others relative to reimbursement of public health dental hygienists.
Financial Services.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to the sustainability of public health dental hygienists through adequate
reimbursements.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 The fifth sentence of the third paragraph of section 51 of chapter 112 is hereby amended
2 by striking out the words “by Medicaid but except as required by federal Medicaid law, shall not
3 seek reimbursement from any other insurance or third party payor”.
