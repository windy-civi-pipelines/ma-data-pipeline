By Representative González of Springfield, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1175) of
Carlos González relative to insurance premiums for homeowners. Financial Services.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 1026 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to provide homeowners with fair insurance premiums.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 5 of chapter 175A of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by inserting after the word “expenses”, in line 26, the following words:-
3 An insurer shall not adjust the rate of a homeowner policy because on a claim of fire,
4 theft, storm or water damage where the insured is found to be not at fault.
