HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 3065 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1176
By Representative González of Springfield, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1176) of
Carlos González relative to homeowners’ insurance complaint education. Financial Services.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 1027 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to homeowners’ insurance complaint education.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 99 of chapter 175 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by adding the following paragraph:-
3 Seventeenth, a company shall print, on a policy or notice of renewal that informs the
4 insured of a premium increase, information related to filing an insurance complaint with the
5 division of insurance, including, but not limited to, the right of an insured to file a complaint
6 regarding a change in premium or policy, phone number and contact information of the division
7 of insurance consumer service unit and webpage of division of insurance where a complaint can
8 be filed online. This information shall be included in the sentence immediately following the
9 sentence containing the updated premium amount.
