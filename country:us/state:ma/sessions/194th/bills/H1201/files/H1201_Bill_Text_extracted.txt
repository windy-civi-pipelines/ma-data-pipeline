HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2244 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1201
By Representative Jones of North Reading, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1201) of
Bradley H. Jones, Jr., and others relative to insurance surcharges for driving with an expired
inspection sticker. Financial Services.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 1058 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to insurance surcharges.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 113B of chapter 175 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official
2 Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after the word “incident” in line 227, the following
3 sentence:- Driving a motor vehicle which has an expired inspection sticker shall not be
4 considered a surchargeable incident.
