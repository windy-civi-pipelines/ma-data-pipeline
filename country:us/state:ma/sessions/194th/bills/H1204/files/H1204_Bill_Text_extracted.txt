HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2248 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1204
By Representative Jones of North Reading, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1204) of
Bradley H. Jones, Jr., and others for legislation to establish pregnancy as a qualifying event for
eligibility for special enrollment in certain health plans in the state health insurance exchange.
Financial Services.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 1052 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act establishing pregnancy as a qualifying event.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, in addition to
2 any qualifying event under federal law, rule, or regulation that makes one eligible for a special
3 enrollment period for enrollment in a qualified health plan in the state health insurance exchange
4 established pursuant to the federal patient protection and affordable care act, the state health
5 insurance exchange shall allow for the enrollment of a pregnant individual within 30 days
6 following the commencement of the pregnancy; provided however, that the pregnancy is
7 certified by a licensed health care provider, as defined in chapter 112 of the General Laws. Upon
8 such enrollment, any qualified health plan in the state health insurance exchange shall ensure that
9 coverage is effective on the first day of the month in which said certification is received.
1 of 2
10 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect on January 1, 2026.
2 of 2
