HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 194 FILED ON: 1/7/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1213
By Representative Kane of Shrewsbury, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1213) of
Hannah Kane and others relative to motor vehicle insurance discounts for marijuana impairment
education courses. Financial Services.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 1063 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to insurance discounts.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding any general or special laws to the contrary, motor vehicle insurance
2 companies shall have the option of offering insurance discounts to licensed operators of motor
3 vehicles who complete a marijuana impairment education course. The course may be offered by
4 accredited driving schools and insurance companies, pending approval from the commissioner of
5 insurance, and the course curriculum must include a module on the dangers of driving under the
6 influence of marijuana.
