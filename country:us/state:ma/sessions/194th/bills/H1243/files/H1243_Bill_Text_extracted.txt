HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2425 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1243
By Representative LeBoeuf of Worcester, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1243) of
David Henry Argosky LeBoeuf relative to homeowners insurance. Financial Services.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to housing rehabilitation grant programs.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 175 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after section 4G the
2 following section:-
3 Section 4H. An insurer licensed to write and engaged in the writing of homeowners
4 insurance in the commonwealth shall extend a residential owner’s coverage by 60 days if the
5 residential owner: (i) received a coverage policy cancellation from the insurer that cited as the
6 reason for cancellation a condition or conditions of the property; and (ii) is approved for a
7 federal, state or local housing rehabilitation grant. The commissioner shall verify that the
8 rehabilitation grant was granted for the purpose of making the necessary repairs to the property
9 to remediate the cited condition or conditions.
