HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 4107 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1385
By Representative Lawn of Watertown, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1385) of
John J. Lawn, Jr., relative to expanding the moral obligation bond program to include acute care
hospitals. Health Care Financing.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act expanding the moral obligation bond program to acute care hospitals.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Paragraph (n 1/2) of section 5 of chapter 614 of the acts of 1968, as inserted
2 by chapter 288 of the acts of 2010, is hereby amended by striking out the words “community
3 hospitals” and inserting in place thereof the following:- “acute care hospitals”.
4 SECTION 2. Subsection (g) of section 10 of chapter 614 of the acts of 1968, as inserted
5 by section 21 of chapter 9 of the acts of 2011, is hereby amended by striking out the words
6 “community hospital” in each instance in which they appear, and inserting in place thereof the
7 following:- “acute care hospital”.
8 SECTION 3. Subsection (g) of section 10 of chapter 614 of the acts of 1968, as inserted
9 by section 21 of chapter 9 of the acts of 2011, is hereby amended by striking out the words
10 “community hospitals” in each instance in which they appear, and inserting in place thereof the
11 following:-- “acute care hospitals”.
1 of 2
12 SECTION 4. Subsection (g) of section 10 of chapter 614 of the acts of 1968, as inserted
13 by section 21 of chapter 9 of the acts of 2011, is hereby amended by striking paragraph (12) in its
14 entirety.
2 of 2
