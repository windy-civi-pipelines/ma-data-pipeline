HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 371 FILED ON: 1/8/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1424
By Representative Cabral of New Bedford, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1424) of
Antonio F. D. Cabral relative to higher education capital investments in gateway municipalities
or abutting municipalities. Higher Education.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to higher education capital investment in gateway cities.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 258, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting
2 the following after section 14: -
3 Section 15. Notwithstanding any special or general law to the contrary, the authorizations
4 contained in this chapter may not be used to fund projects that fail to meet one of the following
5 criteria: (a) construction of the project began prior to July 1, 2021; (b) the project is not located
6 in a municipality that is or is adjacent to a gateway municipality, as defined by section 3A of
7 chapter 23A of the General Laws, or (c) the project is located within a gateway municipality, as
8 so defined, is within 2000 feet of such municipality’s city hall and is or will be within 500 feet
9 of public transportation upon completion of the project.
