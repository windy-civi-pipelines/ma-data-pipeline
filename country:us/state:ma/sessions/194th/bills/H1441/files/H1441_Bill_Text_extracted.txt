HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1418 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1441
By Representative Jones of North Reading, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1441) of
Bradley H. Jones, Jr., and others relative to the acceptance of Advanced Placement scores for
college credit. Higher Education.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 1270 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to acceptance of AP scores for college credit.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 15A of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby
2 amended by adding after section 45 the following section:-
3 Section 46. The department of higher education is hereby directed to promulgate
4 regulations establishing a standard whereby college credit is guaranteed at state colleges and
5 universities for students who achieve an advanced placement exam score of 3 or higher.
