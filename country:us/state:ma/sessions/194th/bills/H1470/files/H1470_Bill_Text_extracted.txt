HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 4067 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1470
By Representative Biele of Boston, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1470) of David
Biele relative to establishing an advisory commission to study the best practices and safety
protocols of state subsidized senior and non-elderly disabled housing. Housing.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 1298 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to senior and non-elderly disabled housing.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Within 45 days after the effective date of this act, a 15-member advisory commission
2 shall be established to study the best practices and safety protocols of state subsidized senior and
3 non-elderly disabled housing. The advisory committee shall consist of the following members:
4 two members selected by the Governor of the Commonwealth; four members selected by the
5 Speaker of the House; four members selected by the Senate President; one member selected by
6 the Citizens Housing and Planning Association; one member selected by the Massachusetts
7 Chapter of the National Association of Housing and Redevelopment Officials; one member
8 selected by the Mass Union of Public Housing Tenants; one member selected by the Disability
9 Law Center; one member selected by the Massachusetts Municipal Association.
