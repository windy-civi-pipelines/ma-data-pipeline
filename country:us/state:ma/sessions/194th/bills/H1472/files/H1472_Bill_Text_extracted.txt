HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1677 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1472
By Representative Cahill of Lynn, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1472) of Daniel
Cahill relative to affordable and public housing options. Housing.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to increasing affordable and public housing options.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 3a of Chapter 40a of the General laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting, after the word “corporation” in line 57, the
3 following words:- or by a housing authority for creation of affordable or public housing purposes
