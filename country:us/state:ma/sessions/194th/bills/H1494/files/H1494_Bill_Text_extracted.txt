HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 4092 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1494
By Representative DeCoste of Norwell, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1494) of
David F. DeCoste relative to rental assistance programs. Housing.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to improve access to rental assistance programs.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Not later than January 1, 2024, the executive office of housing and livable
2 communities shall promulgate regulations pursuant to its authority under sections 3 and 6 of
3 Chapter 23B the General Laws, as appearing in 2022 Official Edition, for the purpose of
4 implementing legal authority for said executive office, its departments and contracted regional
5 administering agencies to accept applications from landlords, on behalf of their tenants, to the
6 Massachusetts Rental Voucher Program, Alternative Housing Voucher Program, Rental
7 Assistance for Families in Transition program, and other similar or succeeding programs as may
8 be established.
