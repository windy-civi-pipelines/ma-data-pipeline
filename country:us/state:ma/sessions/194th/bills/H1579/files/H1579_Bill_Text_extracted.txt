HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 791 FILED ON: 1/13/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1579
By Representative Xiarhos of Barnstable, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1579) of
Steven George Xiarhos, Donald R. Berthiaume, Jr., and Joseph D. McKenna relative to
emergency shelter assistance for commonwealth residents. Housing.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to emergency shelter assistance for commonwealth residents.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 30 of chapter 23B of the General Laws, as amended by section 120
2 of chapter 7 of the acts of 2023, is hereby further amended by adding the following sentence:-
3 Notwithstanding any general or special law, rule, or regulation to the contrary, the
4 emergency housing assistance program established herein shall be available only to residents of
5 the commonwealth who are citizens of the United States.
