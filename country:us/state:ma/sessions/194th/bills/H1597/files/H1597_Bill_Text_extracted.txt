HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 472 FILED ON: 1/9/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1597
By Representative Cabral of New Bedford, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1597) of
Antonio F. D. Cabral relative to the use of prison labor outside of the Commonwealth. The
Judiciary.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 1400 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act limiting the use of prison labor.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 125 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby
2 amended by adding the following section:-
3 Section 22. No administrator or superintendent of any correctional institution, including
4 any correctional facility, county correctional facility, penal institution, state correctional facility,
5 state prison, or jail, shall transfer any inmate or prisoner outside of the commonwealth; and no
6 inmate or prisoner of any correctional institution, including any correctional facility, county
7 correctional facility, penal institution, state correctional facility, state prison, or jail, shall be
8 transferred outside of the commonwealth, for the purpose of engaging in labor outside of the
9 Commonwealth.
