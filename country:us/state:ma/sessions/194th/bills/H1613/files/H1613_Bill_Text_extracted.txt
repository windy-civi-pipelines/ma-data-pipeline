Court assembled:
The undersigned legislators and/or citizens respectfully petition for the adoption of the accompanying bill:
An Act permitting persons to bring car damage arising from a pothole against the public entity
into small claims court.
_______________
PETITION OF:
NAME: DISTRICT/ADDRESS: DATE ADDED:
Tackey Chan 2nd Norfolk 1/13/2025

HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 893 FILED ON: 1/13/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1613
By Representative Chan of Quincy, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1613) of Tackey
Chan for legislation to authorize the filing for damages in small claims court against a public
entity arising from potholes under the jurisdiction of said entity. The Judiciary.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 1413 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act permitting persons to bring car damage arising from a pothole against the public entity
into small claims court.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 3 of Chapter 258 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended in line 6 after the word “county” by inserting the following words:- “and
3 except in the case where the damage to a claimant’s motor vehicle’s tire, rim or both is caused by
4 a state, county or municipal roadway shall be brought under sections 21 to 25 of chapter 218 of
5 the General Laws.”
