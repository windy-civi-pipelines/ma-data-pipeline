HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 3114 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1628
By Representative Cusack of Braintree, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1628) of
Mark J. Cusack for legislation to increase the penalties for continuing to practice a trade or
profession after a certificate, registration, license or authority to do so has been suspended,
revoked or cancelled. The Judiciary.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 1429 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to penalties for unlicensed work.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Subsection (a) of section 65 of chapter 112 of the General Laws, as
2 appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out the word “$2,500” in
3 line 5 and inserting in place thereof, the following word:- $10,000
4 SECTION 2. Subsection (b) of section 65 of chapter 112, as so appearing, is hereby
5 amended by striking out the word “$1,000” in line 13 and inserting in place thereof, the
6 following word:- $5,000
7 SECTION 3. Subsection (b) of section 65 of chapter 112, as so appearing, is hereby
8 amended by striking out the word “$2,500” in line 14 and inserting in place thereof, the
9 following word:- $10,000
1 of 2
10 SECTION 4. Section 65A of chapter 112 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
11 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out the word “$1,000” in line 7 and inserting in
12 place thereof, the following word:- $5,000
13 SECTION 5. Section 65A of chapter 112 of the General Laws, as so appearing, is hereby
14 amended by striking out the word “$2,500” in line 8 and inserting in place thereof, the following
15 word:- $10,000
16 SECTION 6. Section 5 of chapter 141 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
17 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking the first sentence and inserting in place thereof,
18 the following sentence:-
19 Any person, firm or corporation, or employee thereof, and any representative, member or
20 officer of such firm or corporation individually, entering upon or engaging in the business and
21 work hereinbefore defined, without having complied with this chapter, shall for the first offence
22 be punished by a fine not to exceed $5,000, and for a subsequent offence by a fine not to exceed
23 $10,000 or by imprisonment in the house of correction for six months, or both.
24 SECTION 7. Section 3L of chapter 143 of the General Laws, as so appearing in the 2022
25 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking the words “five hundred” in line 31 and inserting
26 in place thereof, the following words:- "ten thousand"
2 of 2
