In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to retirement reporting.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 15 of chapter 32 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by striking out paragraph (5) and inserting in place thereof the following
3 paragraph:-
4 (5) If the attorney general or a district attorney becomes aware of a final conviction of a
5 member of a retirement system under circumstances which may require forfeiture of the
6 member’s rights to a pension, retirement allowance or return of accumulated total deductions
7 pursuant to this chapter, section 58 or 59 of chapter 30 or section 25 of chapter 268A, the
8 attorney general or district attorney shall immediately notify the commission of such conviction;
9 provided, however, that the commission shall not report such conviction to the member’s
10 retirement board or employer unless the commission: (i) has determined that the conviction
1 of 2
11 requires forfeiture of that member’s rights to a pension, retirement allowance or return of
12 accumulated total deductions pursuant to this chapter, section 58 or 59 of chapter 30 or section
13 25 of chapter 268A; and (ii) is directing that member’s retirement board or employer to take
14 action to effectuate such forfeiture.
2 of 2
