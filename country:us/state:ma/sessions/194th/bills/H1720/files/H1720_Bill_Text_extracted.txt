By Representative Garry of Dracut, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1720) of Colleen
M. Garry relative to a court approved parent education program in certain probate proceedings.
The Judiciary.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 1511 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to court approved parent education program.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 209C of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2014 Official Edition, is hereby
2 amended by adding the following:-
3 Section 25. Any parent involved in a paternity case must complete a Probate and Family
4 Court approved parent education program.
