HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1329 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1781
By Representative Jones of North Reading, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1781) of
Bradley H. Jones, Jr., and others relative to the release of sex offender information to victims.
The Judiciary.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 1580 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to the release of sex offender information to victims.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 178I of chapter 6 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by inserting after the second paragraph, the following:-
3 “Upon the request of a victim who has enrolled in the board’s victim services unit, the
4 board shall inform the victim of the sex offender’s final classification, as well as the sex
5 offender’s home address, secondary address, work address and where the offender attends
6 school.”
