HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2529 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1821
By Representative Kushmerek of Fitchburg, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1821)
of Michael P. Kushmerek relative to jury duty parking fees. The Judiciary.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to jury duty parking fees.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 27A of chapter 218 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by adding the following subsection: --
3 j) Citizens who are summoned for jury sessions shall have parking fees, not to exceed
4 $50 per day, reimbursed by the district court.
