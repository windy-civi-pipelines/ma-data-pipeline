HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 3432 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1862
By Representative Mahoney of Worcester, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1862) of
John J. Mahoney relative to car theft with digital devices. The Judiciary.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to car theft with digital devices.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 266 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by inserting after section 49 the following section:-
3 Section 49A. (a) As used in this section, the following words shall have the following
4 meanings:-
5 “Motor vehicle key programming or emulating device”, means any device, computer
6 program, application, or software designed or used to electronically communicate with a motor
7 vehicle for the purpose of programming or emulating a motor vehicle key fob that is capable of
8 being used to unlock or start the motor vehicle.
9 “Relay attack device” means any device, computer program, application, or software
10 designed to intercept the signal from the key fob of a motor vehicle to unlock or start the motor
11 vehicle.
1 of 2
12 (b) No person shall, with intent to steal a motor vehicle or other property therefrom,
13 knowingly manufacture, sell, offer to sell, transfer, or possess a motor vehicle key programming
14 or emulating device or relay attack device.
15 (c) No person shall, with intent to steal a motor vehicle or other property therefrom,
16 knowingly aid or permit another to use a motor vehicle key programming or emulating relay
17 attack advice.
18 (d) Whoever violates this section shall be punished by imprisonment in the state prison
19 for not more than ten years, or by a fine of not more than one thousand dollars and imprisonment
20 in jail for not more than two- and one-half years.
2 of 2
