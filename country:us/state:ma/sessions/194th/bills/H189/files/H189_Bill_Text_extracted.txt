By Representative Vaughn of Wrentham, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 189) of
Marcus S. Vaughn relative to billboard advertisements for cannabis. Cannabis Policy.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 3560 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to billboard advertisements for cannabis.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 4 of chapter 94G of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2020 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by inserting after the word “data”, in line 234, the following words:- ;
3 provided, however, that the commission shall review every billboard or other large public facing
4 advertisement and approve the imagery, size, location, font size, size of the required disclaimers
5 and any other element of such advertisements as determined by the commission.
