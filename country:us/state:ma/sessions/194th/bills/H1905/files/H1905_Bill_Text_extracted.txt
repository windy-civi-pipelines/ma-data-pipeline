HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2411 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1905
By Representative Muradian of Grafton, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1905) of
David K. Muradian, Jr., relative to the indemnification of certain persons from civil proceedings
for injuries or deaths of persons unlawfully entering their dwelling. The Judiciary.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 1692 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to civil liability for protecting one’s home.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 8A of chapter 278 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2012 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by inserting the following paragraph:-
3 If a person using this defense is found not guilty by a court of competent jurisdiction, said
4 person shall not be liable in civil proceedings for the injury or death of the person unlawfully in
5 said dwelling.
