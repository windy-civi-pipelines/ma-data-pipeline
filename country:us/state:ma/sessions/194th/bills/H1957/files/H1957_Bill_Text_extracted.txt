HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1712 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1957
By Representative Rogers of Cambridge, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1957) of
David M. Rogers and Middlesex District Attorney Marian Ryan for legislation to ensure the
ability to prosecute repeat operating under the influence offenses. The Judiciary.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 1735 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to ensure the ability to prosecute repeat OUI offenses.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 24 of chapter 90 of the General Laws is hereby amended by adding, after the last
2 sentence in subsection (1)(c)(4), the following new sentence:- Notwithstanding any contrary
3 provision of section 100A of chapter 276 of the General Laws, any such prior conviction, if
4 otherwise admissible, shall not be inadmissible because it has been sealed pursuant to that
5 section.
