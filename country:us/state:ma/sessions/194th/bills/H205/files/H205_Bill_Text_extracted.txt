HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1497 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 205
By Representative Berthiaume of Spencer, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 205) of
Donald R. Berthiaume, Jr., relative to background checks for kinship foster care. Children,
Families and Persons with Disabilities.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 132 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relating to background checks for kinship foster care.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 32 of Chapter 119 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2018 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by adding at the end thereof the following paragraph:- the department shall
3 screen potential grandparent care givers with a twenty year maximum look back on the potential
4 guardian’s record. This is applicable to any minor infraction that has not been repeated since the
5 first infraction. Instances of felonies, violence, and sexual offenses, no matter when it occurred,
6 will not be removed from the look back.
