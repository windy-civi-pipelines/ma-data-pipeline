HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2088
By Representative Decker of Cambridge, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2088) of
Marjorie C. Decker relative to collective bargaining for teachers and other school employees.
Labor and Workforce Development.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to restore collective bargaining for teachers and other school employees.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 1J of chapter 69 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2020
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out clause (8) of subsection (d); and striking out
3 in clause (9) of subsection (d) the words “of practices” each time they appear; and by striking out
4 subsection (g); and by striking out the period at the end of the first sentence in subsection (i) and
5 adding the words: “subject to Chapter 150E.”; and by striking out in the third paragraph of
6 subsection (k) the second sentence; and by striking out clause (7) of subsection (o); and by
7 striking out in clause (9) of sub-section (o) the words: “or practices” each time they appear; and
8 by striking out the period at the end of the first sentence in subsection (s) and adding the words:
9 “subject to Chapter 150E.”.
10 SECTION 2. Section 1K of chapter 69 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2020
11 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out clause (5) of subsection (d); and by striking
12 out in clause (8) of subsection (d) the words “or practices” each time they appear; and by striking
1 of 2
13 out subsection (e); and by striking out in line 323 of subsection (h) the words: “contracts,
14 collective bargaining agreements or”.
