HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2157
By Representative Plouffe of Brockton, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2157) of
Bridget Plouffe relative to the Massachusetts Medical Society. Labor and Workforce
Development.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act updating the Medical Society mission statement.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 491 of the Acts of 1969, is hereby amended in section 2 by striking the word
2 “citizens” and replacing it with the following word:- people.
