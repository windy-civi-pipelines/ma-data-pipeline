HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 901 FILED ON: 1/13/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2259
By Representative Diggs of Barnstable, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2259) of Kip
A. Diggs relative to authorizing the Cotuit Fire District to raise and appropriate money for the
Historical Society of Santuit and Cotuit. Municipalities and Regional Government.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 5090 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act authorizing the Cotuit Fire District to raise and appropriate money for the Historical
Society of Santuit and Cotuit.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 328 of the acts of 1926, as most recently amended by chapter 26 of the acts of
2 2000, is hereby further amended by inserting after section 2 the following section:-
3 Section 2A. The district may raise, appropriate and transfer funds to The Historical
4 Society of Santuit and Cotuit, Inc. in the city known as the town of Barnstable to provide for its
5 operational needs and programming and preservation services.
