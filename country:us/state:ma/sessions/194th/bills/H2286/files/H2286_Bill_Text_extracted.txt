HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1218 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2286
By Representative Howitt of Seekonk, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2286) of
Steven S. Howitt relative to property subdivision. Municipalities and Regional Government.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 2069 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to property subdivision.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 81L of Chapter 41 of the Massachusetts General Laws, as
2 appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after the word “thereon”
3 in line 15, the following the language:- “, d) and provided that, not more than 3 new lots for
4 development are being created by such division.”
5 SECTION 2. Section 81P of Chapter 41 of the Massachusetts General Laws, as appearing
6 in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking the words “twenty-one days” in line
7 13 and inserting the words “thirty days.”
8 SECTION 3. Section 81P of Chapter 41 of the Massachusetts General Laws, as appearing
9 in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking the words “twenty-one days” in line
10 19 and inserting the words “thirty days.”
