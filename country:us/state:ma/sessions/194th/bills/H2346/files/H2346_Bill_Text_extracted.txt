HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2927 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2346
By Representative Uyterhoeven of Somerville, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No.
2346) of Erika Uyterhoeven relative to municipalities owning and operating broadband high
speed internet infrastructure and services. Municipalities and Regional Government.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to a local option for municipalities to own and operate their own internet
provider.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 40 of the General Laws, as amended by section 9 of chapter 248 of the acts of
2 2024, is here by amended by adding the following section:-
3 Section 71. Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, a city, town,
4 municipality or district that accepts this section in the manner provided in section 4 of chapter 4
5 may own, operate, maintain or manage broadband high speed internet infrastructure and services
6 or hire others to do so on its behalf and may take any reasonable action necessary to establish
7 and operate broadband high speed internet infrastructure and services without establishing a
8 municipal lighting plant.
