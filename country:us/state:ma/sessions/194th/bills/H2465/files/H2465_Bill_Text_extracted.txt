HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2882 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2465
By Representative Kerans of Danvers, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2465) of
Sally P. Kerans relative to athletic trainers and athletic training. Public Health.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to athletic training.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 23A of chapter 112 of the General Laws is hereby amended by
2 striking out the words “and who limits his practice to schools, teams or organizations with whom
3 he is associated” from the definition of "Athletic Trainer."
4 SECTION 2. The Board of Allied Health Professions must review and update the rules
5 and regulations pursuant to this act within 180 days of the passage of this act.
6 SECTION 3. Section 54A of chapter 71 of the general laws, as appearing in the 2022
7 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after the words “eleven C“, in line 3, the
8 following words:- or a licensed athletic trainer, who have completed the annual head injury
9 safety training, as required by 105 CMR 201.007.
10 SECTION 4. Section 8A (b)(7) of chapter 69 is hereby amended by inserting the word
11 “athletic” after “coaches” and before “trainers”. Section 8A (c) of chapter 69 is hereby amended
12 by inserting the word “athletic” after “coaches” and before “trainers”.
