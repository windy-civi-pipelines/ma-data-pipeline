The undersigned legislators and/or citizens respectfully petition for the adoption of the accompanying bill:

NAME: DISTRICT/ADDRESS: DATE ADDED:
John J. Lawn, Jr. 10th Middlesex 1/17/2025

HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 3497 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2476
By Representative Lawn of Watertown, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2476) of
John J. Lawn, Jr. for legislation to finance the Betsy Lehman Center’s statewide provider health
care safety education pilot program. Public Health.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to finance the Betsy Lehman Center’s statewide provider health care safety education
pilot program.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding any general or special rule to the contrary, the treasurer shall transfer a
2 total of $1,500,000 from the Board of Registration in Medicine Trust Fund established in section
3 35M of chapter 10 of the General Laws to the Betsy Lehman Center for Patient Safety and
4 Medical Error Reduction, to support implementation of the roadmap to health care safety for
5 Massachusetts, including a pilot program of electronic health record safety event monitoring and
6 a statewide program of health care safety education.
