HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2220 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2502
By Representatives John Francis Moran of Boston and Montaño of Boston, a petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 2502) of John Francis Moran, Samantha Montaño and others
for legislation to clarify prescription monitoring program activities. Public Health.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to clarify prescription monitoring program activities.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 24A of chapter 94C of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after subsection (m) the following subsection:-
3 (n) Notwithstanding subsection (a)(1), the department shall exclude testosterone from the
4 list of drugs monitored in the prescription drug monitoring system.
5 SECTION 2. Immediately upon passage of this Act, the department shall delete from the
6 prescription monitoring system established under said section 24A any existing data concerning
7 the prescribing and dispensing of testosterone.
