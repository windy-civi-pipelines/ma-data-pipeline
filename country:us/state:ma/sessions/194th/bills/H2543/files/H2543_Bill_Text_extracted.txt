HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 3794 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2543
By Representative Sousa of Framingham, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2543) of
Priscila S. Sousa for legislation to increase the accountability of hospitals that interrupt or divert
required services. Public Health.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 2275 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act requiring hospital to maintain services.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 51 of chapter 111 is hereby amended by adding at the end of the first paragraph,
2 the following new sentence:-
3 “If any licensed facility is unable to provide the services required by said license,
4 requiring the diversion or temporary pause in service of patients on three or more occasions in an
5 calendar year, the Department shall immediately convene a public hearing in the community
6 served by said facility and shall, at the Department’s sole discretion, consider corrective actions,
7 including but not limited to revocation of said facility’s license.”
