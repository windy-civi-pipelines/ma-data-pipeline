HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1279 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2568
By Representative Barber of Somerville, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2568) of
Christine P. Barber, Paul J. Donato and Patrick Joseph Kearney for legislation requiring carbon
monoxide alarms in all residential, governmental and commercial structures. Public Safety and
Homeland Security.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 2292 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act requiring carbon monoxide alarms in all residential, governmental and commercial
structures.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 26F ½ of chapter 148 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2016
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out, in lines 2 and 3, the words “occupied in
3 whole or in part for residential purposes.”
4 SECTION 2. Section 26F ½ of chapter 148 of the General Laws is hereby amended by
5 striking out, in lines 22 and 23, the words “occupied in whole or in part for residential purposes.”
6 SECTION 3. Section 26F ½ of chapter 148 of the General Laws is hereby amended by
7 striking out, in lines 29 and 30, the words “occupied in whole or in part for residential purposes.”
