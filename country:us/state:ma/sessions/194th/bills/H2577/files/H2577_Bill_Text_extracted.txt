HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1951 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2577
By Representative Consalvo of Boston, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2577) of
Rob Consalvo relative to fire safety of state-owned buildings, structures and property. Public
Safety and Homeland Security.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to fire safety of state-owned buildings, structures and property.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 148 is hereby amended by inserting after section 4 the following
2 new section:
3 Section 4A. Applicability of the fire laws and fire code in state-owned buildings:-
4 “The provisions of the state fire code under said chapter 148 of the General Laws, and the
5 rules and regulations promulgated therefrom by the board of fire prevention regulations, shall
6 apply to all buildings, structures and property owned, operated or controlled by the
7 Commonwealth, other than the State House. The Head of the Fire Department, as defined in
8 section 1 of chapter 148, shall enforce the provisions of this chapter and section with respect to
9 all buildings, structures and property owned, operated or controlled by the Commonwealth
10 located within such city, town or district.”
11 SECTION 2. This Act shall take effect upon its passage.
