HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 4144 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2624
By Representative Gómez of Easthampton, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2624) of
Homar Gómez and Patrick Joseph Kearney for legislation to mandate flotation devices in police
vehicles. Public Safety and Homeland Security.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to mandate flotation devices in police vehicles.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 147 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting the following sections:
2 Section XX. Local and state police officers shall be equipped with a flotation device in
3 their on duty vehicles. Said flotation device shall be large enough and suitable for use in a water
4 rescue. These officials shall further receive water rescue training, including, but not limited to,
5 instruction on how to safely and properly use flotation devices in the event of a rescue.
6 SECTION XX. The municipal police training committee may promulgate regulations for
7 purposes of implementing this Act.
