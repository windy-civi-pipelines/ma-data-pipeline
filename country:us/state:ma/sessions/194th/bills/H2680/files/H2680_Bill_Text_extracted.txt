HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 3962 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2680
By Representative Plouffe of Brockton, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2680) of
Bridget Plouffe for legislation to require public safety vehicles to be equipped with emergency
thermal blankets. Public Safety and Homeland Security.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 2298 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act requiring public safety vehicles to be equipped with emergency thermal blankets.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 6A of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after section 18Z the
2 following section:-
3 Section 18AA. Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, all emergency
4 response vehicles shall be equipped with an emergency thermal blanket. For the purposes of this
5 section the term emergency response vehicle shall include, but not be limited to, a fire apparatus,
6 police vehicle, ambulance or disaster vehicle.
