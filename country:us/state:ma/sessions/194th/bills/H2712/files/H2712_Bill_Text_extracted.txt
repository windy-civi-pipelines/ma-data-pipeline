HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 3598 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2712
By Representative Sweezey of Duxbury, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2712) of
Kenneth P. Sweezey relative to pre-ban magazines. Public Safety and Homeland Security.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to pre-ban magazines.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 21 of chapter 135 of the Acts of 2024 is hereby repealed.
2 SECTION 2. Section 22 of chapter 135 of the Acts of 2024 is hereby repealed.
3 SECTION 3. Section 49 of chapter 135 of the Acts of 2024 is hereby repealed.
4 SECTION 4. Section 71 of chapter 135 of the Acts of 2024 is hereby repealed.
