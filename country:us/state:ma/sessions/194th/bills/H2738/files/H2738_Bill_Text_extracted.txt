HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2738
By Representative Xiarhos of Barnstable, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2738) of
Steven George Xiarhos and Joseph D. McKenna relative to providing advanced life support to
police dogs injured in the line of duty. Public Safety and Homeland Security.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 2423 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to providing advanced life support to police dogs injured in the line of duty.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. subsection (b) of Section 9A of Chapter 111C of the General Laws, as most
2 recently amended by Section 2 of Chapter 23 of the Acts of 2022, is hereby further amended by
3 striking out the words "basic level".
4 SECTION 2. Said subsection (b) of Section 9A of Chapter 111C of the General Laws, as
5 most recently amended by Section 2 of Chapter 23 of the Acts of 2022, is hereby further
6 amended by striking out the words "provided, however, that nothing in this section shall
7 authorize the provision of advanced life support care to a police dog;" and inserting in place
8 thereof the following words:- provided, however, that nothing in this section shall be construed
9 to prohibit the provision of advanced life support care to a police dog by a person who is
1 of 2
10 otherwise qualified to render such level of care by virtue of their professional licensing under
11 this chapter and their training under this section;.
2 of 2
