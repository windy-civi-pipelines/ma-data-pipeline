HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 566 FILED ON: 1/10/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2773
By Representative Chan of Quincy, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2773) of Tackey
Chan for legislation to define the word “person” for retirement purposes. Public Service.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 2460 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act defining person for retirement purposes.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 32 of the Massachusetts General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is amended in Section 1 by adding after the definition of "Pension Reserve Fund" the following
3 definition:- "Person", any person, individual or corporation; retirement board or member of a
4 retirement board; or, organization of retirement board members who has an interest in or
5 fiduciary responsibility for the assets of any retirement system.
