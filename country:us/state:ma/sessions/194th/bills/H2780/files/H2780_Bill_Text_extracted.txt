HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 669 FILED ON: 1/10/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2780
By Representative Chan of Quincy, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2780) of Tackey
Chan and David Holway for legislation to designate constitutional officers as employers of the
employees under their jurisdiction. Public Service.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 2476 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to collective bargaining.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 1 of chapter 150E of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by deleting from the first sentence of the definition of “employee” the words
3 “…and the officers and employees within the departments of the state secretary, state treasurer,
4 state auditor and attorney general.”
5 Section 1 of chapter 150E of the General Laws is hereby further amended by adding a
6 new sentence at the end of the definition of “employer” to read “In the case of employees of the
7 state secretary, state treasurer, state auditor and attorney general, the employer shall be the state
8 secretary, state treasurer, state auditor and attorney general respectively.”
