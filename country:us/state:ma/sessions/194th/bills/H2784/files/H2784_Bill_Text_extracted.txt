HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 674 FILED ON: 1/10/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2784
By Representative Chan of Quincy, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2784) of Tackey
Chan and David Holway relative to placing licensed electricians employed by the
Commonwealth in Group 4 of the contributory retirement system for public employees. Public
Service.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 2473 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to the definition of Group 4 of the retirement system.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 The definition of Group 4, as set forth in Chapter 32, Section 3, paragraph (2),
2 subparagraph (g) of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby
3 amended by adding thereto the words:- employees of the Commonwealth and its political
4 subdivisions who are employed as licensed electricians.
