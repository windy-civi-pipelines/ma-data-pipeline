HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1386 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2786
By Representative Chan of Quincy, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2786) of Tackey
Chan and David Holway relative to retirement benefits for compliance officers at the Department
of Fire Services. Public Service.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 2478 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to compliance officers at the Department of Fire Services.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 3 of chapter 32 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting, in line 307, after the words “acts of 1993;” the
3 following words: - Compliance Officers at the Department of Fire Services;
4 SECTION 2. Section 94 of said chapter 32, as so appearing, is hereby amended by
5 inserting, in line 16, after the words “acts of 1993” the following words: - , Compliance Officers
6 at the Department of Fire Services,
7 SECTION 3. Section 94B of said chapter 32, as so appearing, is hereby amended by
8 inserting, in line 9, after the words “fire controlman” the following words: - , Compliance Officer
9 at the Department of Fire Services, .
