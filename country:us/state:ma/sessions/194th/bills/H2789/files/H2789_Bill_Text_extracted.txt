HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1950 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2789
By Representative Consalvo of Boston, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2789) of
Rob Consalvo relative to Group Insurance Commission trust fund. Public Service.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act related to Group Insurance Commission Trust Fund.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 8 of Chapter 32A of the General Laws shall be amended by inserting the
2 following after the last line of the first paragraph: “Said funds appropriated by the legislature
3 along with the contributions made by the active and retired employees shall be deposited into the
4 group commission trust fund to provide the required coverages offered by the commission.”
