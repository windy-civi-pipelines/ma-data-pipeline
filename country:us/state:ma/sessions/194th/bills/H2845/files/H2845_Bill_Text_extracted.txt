HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1520 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2845
By Representative Giannino of Revere, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2845) of
Jessica Ann Giannino relative to leave with pay for certain incapacitated safety employees.
Public Service.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to accrued leave while awaiting a determination of 111F.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 C. 41& 111F is hereby amended by inserting the following at the end of the 2nd
2 paragraph: " In the event that an officer has to use any accrued leave while awaiting a
3 determination of 111F status and the final determination is favorable then any accrued leave used
4 during the period shall be restored to the officer. "
