HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1726 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2859
By Representative Hawkins of Attleboro, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2859) of
James K. Hawkins relative to establishing job permanency for MADOC instructors. Public
Service.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act establishing job permanency for MADOC instructors.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 “Any provisional Industrial Instructor in the Department of Correction will be eligible
2 and be made permanent within their current job title after twelve months of employment within
3 said department.”
4
