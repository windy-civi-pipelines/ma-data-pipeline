HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2472 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2884
By Representative Kilcoyne of Clinton, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2884) of
Meghan K. Kilcoyne relative to providing veteran's preference for certain members of the
National Guard or United States military reserves. Public Service.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to veteran inclusion.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Part 1, Title IV, Chapter 31, Section 26, of the Massachusetts General Laws is hereby
2 amended by striking out in line 7 of paragraph 1 "(4) all others," and inserting the words " (4)
3 member of the Massachusetts National Guard or Reserves of the United States Armed Forces
4 with no less than two years of continuous service; (5) all others,"
