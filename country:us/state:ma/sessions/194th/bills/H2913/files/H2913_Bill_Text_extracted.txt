HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 3486 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2913
By Representative McMurtry of Dedham, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2913) of
Paul McMurtry relative to public safety line of duty benefits. Public Service.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 2601 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to public safety line of duty benefits.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Subsection (c) of section 100A of chapter 32, as appearing in the 2014 Official Edition, is
2 hereby amended by striking out, in line 15, the words “and who is not subject to chapter 152”,
3 and inserting at the end of said subsection the following sentence:- “The family of a deceased
4 public safety employee, who is subject to chapter 152, shall have the option to receive either the
5 killed in the line of duty benefit or to receive benefits pursuant to chapter 152.”
