HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 711 FILED ON: 1/12/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2931
By Representative Pease of Westfield, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2931) of
Kelly W. Pease relative to earnings for Michael Sacco for calendar year 2023. Public Service.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to earnings limitations for Michael Sacco for calendar year 2023.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 1. Notwithstanding the provisions of section 91b of chapter 32 of the General
2 Laws or any other general or special law to the contrary, for calendar year 2023, the amount of
3 excess earnings attributed to Michael Sacco, former Chief Executive Officer of the Worcester
4 Regional Retirement Board, shall not exceed the total amount paid to him by the Worcester
5 Regional Retirement System in that calendar year, specifically $4,862.84.
6 Section 2. This act shall apply only to Michael Sacco and solely with respect to earnings
7 accrued and retirement payments received during calendar year 2023.
8 Section 3. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
1 of 1
