HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 3960 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2937
By Representative Plouffe of Brockton, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2937) of
Bridget Plouffe that school police officers of the city of Brockton be included in Group 4 of the
contributory retirement system for public employees. Public Service.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 2458 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to Brockton school police.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 3 of Chapter 32 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2016 Official Edition, is
2 hereby amended by inserting after the word “Authority,” in line 294, the following words:-
3 Brockton School Police.
