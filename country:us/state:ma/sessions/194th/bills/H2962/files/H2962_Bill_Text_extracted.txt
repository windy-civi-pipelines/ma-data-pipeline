SEE HOUSE, NO. 2656 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to the commonwealth's fire departments.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 100 of chapter 41 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2018
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after the word “law” in line 31 the following
3 words: - This section shall also apply to any permanent crash crewman, crash boatman, fire
4 controlman or assistant fire controlman employed at the General Edward Lawrence Logan
5 International Airport, members of the Massachusetts military reservation fire department and
6 members of the 104th fighter wing fire department and, for the purposes of this section, the
7 Massachusetts Port Authority, the Massachusetts military reservation and the Barnes Air
8 National Guard Base shall be fire districts.
9 SECTION 2. Section 100B of said chapter 41, as so appearing, is hereby amended by
10 adding the following paragraph:
1 of 2
11 This section shall also apply to any permanent crash crewman, crash boatman, fire
12 controlman or assistant fire controlman from the General Edward Lawrence Logan International
13 Airport, members of the Massachusetts military reservation fire department and members of the
14 104th fighter wing fire department who are retired under a general or special law specifically
15 relating to retirement for accidental disability and, for the purposes of this section, the
16 Massachusetts Port Authority, the Massachusetts military reservation and the Barnes Air
17 National Guard Base shall be fire districts.
18 SECTION 3. Section 94 of chapter 32 of the General Laws, as so appearing, is hereby
19 amended by inserting after the words “into such service” in line 17, the following words:- or, for
20 members of the 104th fighter wing fire department or members of the Massachusetts military
21 reservation fire department, passed a physical examination on entry into service for those
22 departments before they were transitioned to state employment,
23 SECTION 4. Section 94A of said chapter 32, is hereby amended by inserting after the
24 words “into such service” in line 12, the following words:- or, for members of the 104th fighter
25 wing fire department or members of the Massachusetts military reservation fire department,
26 passed a physical examination on entry into service for those departments before they were
27 transitioned to state employment,
28 SECTION 5. Section 94B of said chapter 32, is hereby amended by inserting after the
29 words “into such service” in line 13, the following words:- or, for members of the 104th fighter
30 wing fire department or members of the Massachusetts military reservation fire department,
31 passed a physical examination on entry into service for those departments before they were
32 transitioned to state employment,
2 of 2
