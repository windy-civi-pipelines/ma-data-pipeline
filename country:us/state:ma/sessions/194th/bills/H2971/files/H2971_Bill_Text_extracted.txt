HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 3047 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2971
By Representative Soter of Bellingham, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2971) of
Michael J. Soter and others for legislation to provide public safety telecommunicators with the
same internal benefits as first responders. Public Service.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 2667 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to provide public safety telecommunicators with the same internal benefits as first
responders.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding any general or special law or rule or regulation to the contrary, all
2 Public Safety Dispatchers or Public Safety Telecommunicators shall receive the same internal
3 benefits as First Responders. For the purpose of this paragraph, " Public Safety Dispatchers or
4 Public Safety Telecommunicators" means an individual employed by a public safety agency, as
5 the initial first responder, whose primary responsibility is to receive, process, transmit, or
6 dispatch emergency and nonemergency calls for law enforcement, fire, emergency medical, and
7 other public safety services by telephone, radio, or other communication device, and includes an
8 individual who promotes from this position and supervises individuals who perform these
9 functions. No change shall be made to such Telecommunicators' compensation and pension
10 benefits.
