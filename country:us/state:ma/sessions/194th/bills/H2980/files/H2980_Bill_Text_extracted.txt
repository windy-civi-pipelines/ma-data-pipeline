HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 471 FILED ON: 1/9/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2980
By Representative Ultrino of Malden, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2980) of
Steven Ultrino relative to retirement benefits for county correctional nurses. Public Service.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to retirement benefits for county correctional nurses.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 3 of chapter 32 of the General Laws, as amended by section 27 of
2 chapter 39 of the acts of 2021, is hereby further amended by inserting, in line 274, after the word
3 “facilities”, the following words: - and except any position explicitly classified in another group.
4 SECTION 2. Said section 3 of said chapter 32, as so amended, is hereby further amended
5 by inserting, in line 331, after the words “assistant deputy superintendent”, the following words:-
6 registered nurse, medication-assisted treatment nurse manager, psychiatric nurse, psychology
7 nurse practitioner, director of nursing, licensed practical nurse, nurse practitioner or paramedic.
