HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 133 FILED ON: 1/6/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2994
By Representative Williams of Springfield, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2994) of
Bud L. Williams relative to public employee retirement and pensions for certain veterans. Public
Service.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 2684 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to veterans retirement and pensions.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 32: Section 60, is hereby amended by striking the following:
2 No veteran whose employment first begins after June thirtieth, nineteen hundred and
3 thirty-nine, shall be subject to the provisions of sections fifty-six to fifty-nine, inclusive; nor shall
4 any veteran whose employment first began on or before said June thirtieth be subject to said
5 provisions unless at the time of his retirement the total period of his creditable service is at least
6 equal to twice the time he was not in the employ of the commonwealth or of a county, city, town,
7 district, authority or redevelopment authority subsequent to the date when his employment by the
8 commonwealth or by a county, city, town, district, housing authority or redevelopment authority
9 first began.
