HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 750 FILED ON: 1/13/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2997
By Representative Xiarhos of Barnstable, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2997) of
Steven George Xiarhos that certain employees of the Massachusetts Port Authority be included
in Group 4 of the contributory retirement system for public employees. Public Service.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 2685 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to Mass Port police officers.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 3 of Chapter 32 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2018 Official Editions,
2 is hereby further amended by inserting in the description of Group 4 in paragraph (g) of
3 subdivision (2), in line 268, after the words fire control men; the following:--- employees of the
4 Massachusetts port authority who are employed as port officers and supervisors of said
5 employees who shall include port sergeants, lieutenants, captains, assistant chiefs, deputy chiefs,
6 directors;
1 of 1
