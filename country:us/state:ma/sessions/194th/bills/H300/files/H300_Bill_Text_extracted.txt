HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 3793 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 300
By Representative González of Springfield, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 300) of
Carlos González relative to the membership of the Massachusetts Development Finance Agency
board of directors. Community Development and Small Businesses.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act improving micro business and small business representation.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Subsection (b) of section 2 of chapter 23G of the General Laws, as
2 amended by section 126 of chapter 7 of the acts of 2023, is hereby amended by striking the
3 figure “9” and inserting in place thereof the figure “12”.
4 SECTION 2. Subsection (b) of section 2 of chapter 23 of the General Laws, as amended
5 by section 126 of chapter 7 of the acts of 2023, is hereby amended by inserting after the word
6 “financing” the following:-
7 , 1 of whom shall be experienced in supporting the growth of businesses owned by
8 individuals from diverse communities, 1 of whom shall be the owner of a microbusiness, 1 of
9 whom shall be the owner of a small business
10
