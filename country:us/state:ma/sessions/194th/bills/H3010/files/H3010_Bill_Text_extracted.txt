HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2030 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3010
By Representative Arena-DeRosa of Holliston, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No.
3010) of James C. Arena-DeRosa relative to excluding the Segal AmeriCorps Educational
Award from taxable income. Revenue.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act excluding the Segal Americorps Educational Award from taxable income.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 3 of Chapter 62 of the General Laws is hereby amended in
2 paragraph (a) of Subsection B by adding the following subparagraph:-
3 (21) Any amount received as part of the Segal AmeriCorps Education Award, as
4 stipulated in subtitle D of title I of the National and Community Service Act of 1990. The Segal
5 AmeriCorps Education Award is available to individuals who complete a term of service in the
6 AmeriCorps program. The education award can be used to pay education costs at eligible
7 institutions of higher learning including technical schools, for educational training, or to repay
8 qualified student loans, as determined eligible by the federal government.
9 SECTION 2. This law shall become effective 180 days after its passage.
