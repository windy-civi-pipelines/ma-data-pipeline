HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2211 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3023
By Representative Ayers of Quincy, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3023) of Bruce
J. Ayers for legislation to exempt small charities from the tax on raffle proceeds. Revenue.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 2699 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to exemptions for small charities for tax upon raffle proceeds.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 7A of Chapter 271 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after the
2 word “proceeds”, in line 112, the following words:— over $12,000.
