HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 4080 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3028
By Representative Biele of Boston, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3028) of David
Biele for legislation to further regulate the room occupancy excise. Revenue.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act establishing a friends and family rate.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 1 of Chapter 64G, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is
2 hereby amended by adding after the words “the commissioner of revenue” the following:-
3 “Friends and Family Rate”, rooms rented to employees of an operator of a hotel or motel,
4 and relatives and friends of employees at a rate determined by the operator.
5 SECTION 2. Section 3 of Chapter 64G, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is
6 hereby amended by adding after the words “$15 per day or its equivalent” the following:-
7 An excise for rooms rented under a friends and family rate shall be based solely on the
8 rate determined by the operator.
