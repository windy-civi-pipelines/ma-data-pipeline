HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 3169 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3068
By Representative Cusack of Braintree, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3068) of
Mark J. Cusack relative to delinquent and deferred interest rate uniformity. Revenue.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to delinquent and deferred interest rate uniformity.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 57 of chapter 59 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by striking out, in line 12, the figure “14” and inserting in place thereof the
3 following figure:- 8.
4 Section 57C of said chapter 59, as so appearing, is hereby amended by striking out, in
5 line 155, the word “fourteen” and inserting in place thereof the following figure:- 8.
6 Section 68 of chapter 60, as so appearing, is hereby amended by striking out, in line 9,
7 the word “sixteen” and inserting in place thereof the following figure:- 8.
