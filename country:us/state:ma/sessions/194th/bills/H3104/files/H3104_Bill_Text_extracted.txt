HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3104
By Representative Garry of Dracut, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3104) of Colleen
M. Garry relative to making assisted living programs eligible for tax increment financing and
allowing municipalities to provide flexible targeted incentives. Revenue.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 2806 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to the assisted living programs.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding any special or general law, or rule or regulation to the contrary, an
2 assisted living program shall be eligible through Economic Assistance Coordinating Council for
3 a Tax Increment Financing allowing municipalities to provide flexible targeted incentives to
4 stimulate job-creating development.
