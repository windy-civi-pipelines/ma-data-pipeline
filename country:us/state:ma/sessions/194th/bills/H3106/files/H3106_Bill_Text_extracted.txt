HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 988 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3106
By Representative Gentile of Sudbury, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3106) of
Carmine Lawrence Gentile and Patrick Joseph Kearney for legislation to allow cities and towns
to increase the local tax rate on meals. Revenue.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 2858 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to allow cities and towns to increase the local tax rate on meals.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 2 of chapter 64L of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by inserting after the word “cent”, in line 4, the following words:- or 1.5 per
3 cent.
