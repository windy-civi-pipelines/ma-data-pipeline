HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3124
By Representative Higgins of Leominster, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3124) of
Natalie M. Higgins and others relative to a sales tax exemption for the sale of used books and
other items sold by libraries for fundraising purposes. Revenue.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 2825 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act in aid of libraries.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 6 of chapter 64H of the General Laws as appearing in the 2016 Official Edition is
2 hereby amended by adding at the end thereof the following paragraph:-
3 (yy) used books and other items sold by libraries and library friends groups for the
4 purposes of raising money to be used for library purposes.
