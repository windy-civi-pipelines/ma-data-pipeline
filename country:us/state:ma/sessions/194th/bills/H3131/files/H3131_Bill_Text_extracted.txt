_______________
An Act incentivizing veterans to reside in the Commonwealth.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Clause Twenty-second H of section 5 of chapter 59 of the General Laws, as appearing in
2 the 2020 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out, in line 946, the words “5
3 consecutive years” and inserting in place thereof the following words:- 1 year.
