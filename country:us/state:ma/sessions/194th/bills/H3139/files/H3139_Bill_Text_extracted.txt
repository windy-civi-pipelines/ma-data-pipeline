HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2650 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3139
By Representative Howitt of Seekonk, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3139) of
Steven S. Howitt relative to transparency in property deed requests. Revenue.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act ensuring transparency in property deed requests.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 62C of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby
2 amended by adding, after Section 64, the following new section:-
3 Section 64A. The secretary of state or the register of deeds in a county shall maintain a
4 record of all requests to access a property deed. The secretary of state or register of deeds shall
5 notify a property owner when a record request has been made on the property owner’s deed.
6 Such notification shall be made through mail to the property no later than 10 business days after
7 the deed has been requested.
