HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1244 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3176
By Representative MacGregor of Boston, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3176) of
William F. MacGregor relative to the electronic filing of certain forms used in property
valuation. Revenue.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to the electronic filing of certain forms used in property valuation.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 59 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after
2 section 52C the following new section:-
3 Electronic filing requirements
4 Section 52D. Any form, return or document required or permitted to be filed by the
5 owner or lessee of any real or personal property under this chapter, including sections 5, 5C, 29,
6 38D, 38F, 59 or 61A shall be filed with or transmitted to the board of assessors in such manner,
7 format and medium, including electronic, as the board of assessors shall from time to time
8 prescribe. Failure by the owner or lessee, without reasonable cause, to submit the filing in the
9 manner, format and medium sought by the board of assessors shall not be deemed to have been
10 filed. Reasonable cause shall include reasons beyond the control of the owner or lessee and good
11 faith attempts to comply.
