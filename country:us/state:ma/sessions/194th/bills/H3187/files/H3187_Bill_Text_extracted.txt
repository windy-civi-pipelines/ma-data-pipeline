HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2548 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3187
By Representative McMurtry of Dedham, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3187) of
Paul McMurtry relative to motion pictures filmed in the Commonwealth. Revenue.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 2889 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act recognizing film production in Massachusetts.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Subsection (l) of section 6 of chapter 62 of the General Laws, as appearing
2 in the 2014 Official Edition, is hereby amended by adding the following paragraph:
3 (9) A motion picture shall include a credit and a logo, approved by the commissioner in
4 consultation with the Massachusetts film office, stating that the motion picture was filmed in
5 Massachusetts and displaying a logo designed and approved by the Massachusetts film office.
6 The credit shall include the following title: “Proudly Made in Massachusetts”.
7 SECTION 2. Section 38X of chapter 63 of the General Laws is hereby amended by
8 adding the following subsection:
9 (h) A motion picture shall include a credit and a logo, approved by the commissioner in
10 consultation with the Massachusetts film office, stating that the motion picture was filmed in
1 of 2
11 Massachusetts and displaying a logo designed and approved by the Massachusetts film office.
12 The credit shall include the following title: “Proudly Made in Massachusetts”.
