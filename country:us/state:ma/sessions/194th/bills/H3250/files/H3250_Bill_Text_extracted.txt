HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 4223 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3250
By Representative Soter of Bellingham, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3250) of
Michael J. Soter and others relative to income tax relief for senior citizens. Revenue.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to income tax relief for senior citizens.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 62 of the General Laws of Massachusetts is hereby amended by inserting the
2 following subsection after Section 3(B)(a)(20):
3 "(21) In the case of an individual who has attained the age of 65 years on or before the
4 last day of the taxable year, an amount equal to $50,000."
