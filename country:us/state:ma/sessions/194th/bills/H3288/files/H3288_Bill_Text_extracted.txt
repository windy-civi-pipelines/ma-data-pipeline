HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2155 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3288
By Representative Ayers of Quincy, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3288) of Bruce
J. Ayers for the issuance by the Governor of an annual proclamation setting apart the month of
October as adopt a shelter dog awareness month. State Administration and Regulatory
Oversight.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 2984 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act designating the month of October as adopt a shelter dog month.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 6 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after Section 15TTTTT,
2 the following section:
3 Section 15UUUUU. The governor shall annually issue a proclamation setting apart the
4 month of October as Adopt a Shelter Dog Month, to increase public awareness and encourage
5 the adoption of shelter and rescue dogs in Massachusetts, and recommend that the day be
6 observed in an appropriate manner by the people.
