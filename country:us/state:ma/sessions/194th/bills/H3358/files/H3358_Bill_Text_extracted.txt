HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1080 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3358
By Representatives Hunt of Boston and Biele of Boston, a petition (accompanied by bill, House,
No. 3358) of Daniel J. Hunt and David Biele for the issuance of an annual proclamation by the
Governor to designate the last full week in April as building trades recovery week. State
Administration and Regulatory Oversight.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 3058 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act establishing building trades recovery week.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 6 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after section 15IIIIII the
2 following section:-
3 Section 15JJJJJJ. The governor shall annually issue a proclamation setting apart the last
4 week in April as Building Trades Recovery Week, for the Buildings Trades Employers
5 Association to promote awareness of the dangers of opioids and substance misuse among
6 workers in the construction industry and facilitate discussions and events to combat the opioid
7 epidemic and promote mental health in the commonwealth's building construction industry.
