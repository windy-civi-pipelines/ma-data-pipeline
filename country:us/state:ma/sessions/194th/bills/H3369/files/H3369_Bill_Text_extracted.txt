HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1229 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3369
By Representative Kilcoyne of Clinton, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3369) of
Meghan K. Kilcoyne relative to bids for school transportation contracts that include electric
vehicles. State Administration and Regulatory Oversight.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to school transportation contracts.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 5 of chapter 30B of the General Laws, as appearing in Official Edition, is hereby
2 amended by inserting after the word “bidder” in 96, the following:-
3 ; provided however, for bids for school transportation contracts that include electric
4 vehicles, the procurement officer shall consider all criteria set forth in the invitation for bids
5 when awarding said contract
