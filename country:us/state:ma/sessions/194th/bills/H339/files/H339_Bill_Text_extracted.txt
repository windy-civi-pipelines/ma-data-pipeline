HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 422 FILED ON: 1/9/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 339
By Representative Chan of Quincy, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 339) of Tackey
Chan relative to funeral directors. Consumer Protection and Professional Licensure.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 4475 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to funeral directors.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 83 of chapter 112 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting at the end thereof the following new paragraph:-
3 “All establishments and branches thereof for the preparation, disposition and care of dead
4 human bodies shall participate in the program described in section 9 of chapter 117A as a
5 condition for continued licensure.”
6 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect on December 31, 2027.
