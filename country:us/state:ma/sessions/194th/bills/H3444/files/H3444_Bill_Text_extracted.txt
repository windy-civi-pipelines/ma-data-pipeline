HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2050 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3444
By Representative Arena-DeRosa of Holliston, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No.
3444) of James C. Arena-DeRosa relative to bids or proposals for the installation, repair, or
maintenance of security systems. Telecommunications, Utilities and Energy.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to s-license compliance.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 57 of Chapter 147 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2018 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by inserting after the second sentence the following sentence:-
3 Any bid or proposal for the installation, repair, or maintenance of security systems that is
4 submitted by a person, firm or corporation not in compliance with this section at the time the bid
5 or proposal is submitted shall be deemed invalid.
