HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2768 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3566
By Representative Saunders of Belchertown, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3566)
of Aaron L. Saunders relative to the use of surety bonds by certain utilities.
Telecommunications, Utilities and Energy.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to surety bonds.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 25A of chapter 166 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official
2 Edition, is hereby amended by inserting, in line 67, after the word “attachment”, the following :-
3 “A utility shall not compel as a condition of attachment a licensee, that is a municipal
4 light plant established solely for the purposes of providing telecommunication services,
5 established pursuant to Chapter 164, to incur costs, direct or indirect, including, but not limited
6 to the production of a surety bond, in excess of such rates for attachment as determined by said
7 department pursuant to the provisions of this section.”
