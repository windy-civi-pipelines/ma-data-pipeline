HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2847 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3569
By Representative Saunders of Belchertown, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3569)
of Aaron L. Saunders relative to carbon emission reduction and advanced nuclear energy
generation. Telecommunications, Utilities and Energy.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to carbon emission reduction and advanced nuclear energy generation.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. (a) Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary the
2 department of energy resources shall investigate and report on the development of advanced
3 nuclear energy generation in the Commonwealth.
4 (b) Said report shall include, but not be limited to:
5 1. Impacts and opportunities for the Commonwealth as a result of public law 118–67, the
6 so-called ADVANCE Act
7 2. Safety and environmental considerations associated with siting, construction, and
8 operation of advanced nuclear energy generation
9 3. Economic impacts, including potential tax revenue, job creation during construction
10 and operation of an advanced nuclear facility
1 of 2
11 4. Opportunities to offset carbon emissions as a result of increased use of advanced
12 nuclear energy generation
13 5. Interconnection, transmission, distribution and other grid related considerations
14 necessary for implementation of advanced nuclear energy generation
15 (c) Said report shall be filed with the respective clerks of the House and Senate, and the
16 House and Senate chairs of the Joint Committee on Telecommunications, Utilities, and Energy
17 no later than December 31, 2026
18 SECTION 2. The first sentence of section 105 of said chapter 239, as so appearing, is
19 hereby amended by striking out the following words:- commenced commercial operation before
20 January 1, 2011.
2 of 2
