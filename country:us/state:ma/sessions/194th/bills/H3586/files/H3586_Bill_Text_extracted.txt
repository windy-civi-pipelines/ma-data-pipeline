HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3586
By Representative Biele of Boston, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3586) of David
Biele relative to procurement services for artists. Tourism, Arts and Cultural Development.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 3240 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to procurement services for artists.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 30B Section 1 (b) of the General Laws is hereby amended by
2 inserting the following subsection -
3 (35) any contracts or agreements to repair, purchase or commission artistic services,
4 works of art, public art, and/or artifacts of a unique or intrinsic artistic character.
5 SECTION 2. Chapter 30B Section 2 of the General Laws is hereby amended by
6 inserting-
7 Artistic services (i) Services provided by an individual or group of individuals who
8 profess and practice a skill in the artistic disciplines such as but not limited to the visual arts,
9 literary arts, performing arts, craft-based arts, traditional arts, textile/fiber arts, design,
1 of 2
10 digital/web based art forms, functional arts, media based arts, and any other new or emerging art
11 form.
2 of 2
