HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 3728 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3604
By Representative Arciero of Westford, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3604) of
James Arciero, Scott Hyder and Colleen M. Garry relative to 988 Samaritan suicide prevention
signs on bridges. Transportation.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to 988 Samaritan suicide prevention signs on bridges of the commonwealth.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1 Notwithstanding any general or special law, rule or regulation to the
2 contrary, the department of highways shall install signage on all bridges of the commonwealth
3 under its jurisdiction. There shall be two signs on each bridge at either end of said bridges. The
4 signs shall contain "988 Suicide Prevention" and any other information necessary to convey to
5 individuals that an alternative to suicide exists and help is available by calling said number.
6 Such signs shall be of a reasonable size that is easily readable by either pedestrians or drivers
7 using the bridge as determined by the department of highways.
8 SECTION 2 Signage on all commonwealth bridges shall be installed by the
9 department of highways within one year of the passage of this act.
