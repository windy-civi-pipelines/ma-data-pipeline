HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 3631 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3629
By Representative Blais of Deerfield, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3629) of
Natalie M. Blais and Joanne M. Comerford relative to the permitting of certain trucks and trailer-
trucks for travel on public ways. Transportation.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 3271 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act facilitating cost efficient transportation.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 The first sentence of section 19A of chapter 90 of the General Laws is hereby amended
2 by inserting after the figure “eighty-five” the following words:- ; provided, that a truck or other
3 motor vehicle in combination with a trailer with a total weight equal to or less than 99,000
4 pounds may travel on a public way with such a permit.
