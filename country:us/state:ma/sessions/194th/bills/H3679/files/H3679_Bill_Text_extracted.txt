HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2443 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3679
By Representative Hawkins of Attleboro, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3679) of
James K. Hawkins relative to license plate year of manufacture registration plates when used on
antique automobiles. Transportation.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 3333 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to license plate year of manufacture registration.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding any general or special law or regulation to the contrary, Section 6 of
2 Chapter 90 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2016 official edition, shall be amended by
3 inserting after the word “section” in line 42 the following:-
4 "However, the restoration of year of manufacture registration plates, painted and restored
5 to their original year of manufacture colors and design, will not be considered altering such
6 registration plates when used on antique automobiles."
