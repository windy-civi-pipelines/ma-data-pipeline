HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2653 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3699
By Representative Howitt of Seekonk, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3699) of
Steven S. Howitt and Susannah M. Whipps relative to a vehicle collector license plate.
Transportation.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act establishing a vehicle collector license plate.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 6 of Chapter 90 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by adding the following:-
3 “Notwithstanding any general or special law or rule to the contrary, the registrar shall
4 authorize the registration of a motor vehicle with a single plate attached to the rear, rather than a
5 plate on the front as well as the rear, provided that it is a distinctive registration plate as defined
6 under section 2F. The registrar may authorize an additional charge of not more than $50 for such
7 a plate.”
