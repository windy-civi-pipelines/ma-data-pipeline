HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2480 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3715
By Representative Kilcoyne of Clinton, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3715) of
Meghan K. Kilcoyne and Priscila S. Sousa relative to school bus transportation professionals.
Transportation.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to school bus transportation professionals.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 24 of chapter 151A, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended
2 by adding the following paragraph:
3 (d) Notwithstanding any other provision of this title to the contrary, individuals with a
4 definite return-to-work date that is within twelve (12) weeks of their last day of physical work, as
5 certified by their employer on the employer separation notice provided to the department, shall
6 be exempt from the work search requirements.
