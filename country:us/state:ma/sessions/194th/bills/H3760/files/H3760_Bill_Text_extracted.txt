HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 4126 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3760
By Representative Pease of Westfield, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3760) of
Kelly W. Pease relative to expired motor vehicle inspection stickers. Transportation.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to motor vehicle inspections.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 2 of chapter 90 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended, in line 400, by inserting after the word “registration”, the
3 first time it appears, the following:-
4 “; provided, however, that the registrar shall immediately suspend the registration of a
5 motor vehicle or trailer if its inspection sticker has been expired for more than 60 days, pursuant
6 to the provisions of section 7A”
7 SECTION 2. Section 7A of said chapter 90, as so appearing, is hereby amended by
8 inserting at the end of the first paragraph the following:-
9 “The registration issued to any motor vehicle or trailer which has been operating for more
10 than 60 days with an expired inspection sticker shall be immediately suspended by the registrar
11 until such time as the motor vehicle or trailer is properly inspected and passes its inspection.”
