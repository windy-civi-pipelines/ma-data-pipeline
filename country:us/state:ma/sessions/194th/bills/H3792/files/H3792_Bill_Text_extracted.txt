HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1303 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3792
By Representatives Ultrino of Malden and Howard of Lowell, a petition (accompanied by bill,
House, No. 3792) of Steven Ultrino and Vanna Howard relative to license reinstatement fees for
drivers without a conviction. Transportation.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to license reinstatement fees for drivers without conviction.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 33 of chapter 90 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2020
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out paragraph 36 and inserting in place thereof
3 the following paragraph:-
4 (36) (a) For the reinstatement of any license or right to operate a motor vehicle which has
5 been suspended or revoked pursuant to subsection (a) of section 22, the fee shall be $500.
6 (b) For the reinstatement of any license or right to operate a motor vehicle which has
7 been suspended or revoked pursuant to subsection (e) of section 22, sections 22F, 23, 24, except
8 as otherwise provided below, section 24B, 24D, 24G, 24L or section 34J, and section 28 of
9 chapter 266, the fee shall be $500. The fee for reinstatement following revocation pursuant to
10 subparagraph (2) of paragraph (c) of subdivision (1) of said section 24 shall be $700 and the fee
11 for such reinstatement following a revocation pursuant to subparagraphs (3) and (3 1/2) of said
12 paragraph (c) of said subdivision (1) of said section 24 shall be $1,200; provided, however, that
1 of 2
13 no person shall be made to pay a fee pursuant to this subparagraph unless said person was
14 convicted, placed on probation, otherwise entered a plea of guilty to or admitted to a finding of
15 sufficient facts of said subsection (e) of said section 22, said sections 22F, 23, 24, 24B, 24D,
16 24G, 24L or said section 34J, or said section 28 of said chapter 266.
17 (c) The fee for reinstatement of any license or right to operate a motor vehicle which has
18 been suspended or revoked pursuant to any general or special law shall be $100, but the fee for
19 reinstatement for suspensions and revocation pursuant to subsection (c) of section 22 shall be
20 commensurate with the fee established for the corresponding Massachusetts offense resulting in
21 the suspension or revocation pursuant to the General Laws.
