HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1896 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3793
By Representatives Ultrino of Malden and Howard of Lowell, a petition (accompanied by bill,
House, No. 3793) of Steven Ultrino and Vanna Howard for legislation to authorize
Massachusetts fallen firefighter memorial motorcycle license plates. Transportation.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to authorize Massachusetts Fallen Firefighter Memorial motorcycle license plates.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 90 of the Massachusetts General Laws, as appearing in the 2016
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after Section 6C the following:-
3 Section 6D. The registrar, upon payment of the fee as required in section 33, shall furnish
4 a special distinctive Firefighters Memorial Motorcycle License Plate for use on privately owned
5 motorcycles. Said motorcycle license plate shall be comparable to the current Firefighters
6 Memorial License Plate for automobiles established in 2006.
7 Of the required fee provided in section 33, twenty-eight dollars of the forty dollar special
8 plate fee shall go to support the Massachusetts Fallen Firefighter Memorial and twelve dollars to
9 the MASSDOT for manufacturing the plate. The issuance and renewal of such Firefighter
10 Memorial Motorcycle License Plate shall be collected by the registrar for the funding of the
11 Statewide Memorial located at Ashburton Park at the Massachusetts State House which shall be
1 of 2
12 coordinated by the Massachusetts Fallen Firefighter Memorial charitable, a non-profit 501(C)(3)
13 corporation in accordance with section 2f(c).
14 SECTION 2. This act shall be known as the Dave Condon Firefighter Memorial License
15 Plate Act.
