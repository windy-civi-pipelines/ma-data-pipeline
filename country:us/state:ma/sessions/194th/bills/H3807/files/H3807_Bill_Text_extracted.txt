Transportation.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 3468 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to out of state operator’s motor vehicle license applications.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 The first paragraph of section 8 of chapter 90 of the General Laws, as appearing in the
2 2020 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after the second sentence the following
3 sentence:- The registrar’s examination of an applicant who is surrendering an operator’s license
4 issued by another state or U.S. territory shall include the successful completion of the
5 examination and driving test for a junior operator’s license provided in clause (d) of the second
6 paragraph of this section.
