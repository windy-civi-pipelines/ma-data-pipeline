HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 3638 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3819
By Representative Arciero of Westford, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3819) of
James Arciero and others relative to no cost hunting and fishing licenses for 100 percent disabled
veterans. Veterans and Federal Affairs.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 3481 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to 100% disabled veteran recreation.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding any special or general law, rule or regulation to the contrary, any
2 Massachusetts resident who is a 100 percent disabled veteran shall be entitled to any hunting or
3 fishing license offered by the Commonwealth without fee or cost.
