HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1306 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3854
By Representative Jones of North Reading, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3854) of
Bradley H. Jones, Jr., and others relative to establishing a Vietnam War commemoration fund.
Veterans and Federal Affairs.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 3510 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act establishing a Vietnam War commemoration fund.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 (a) Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, there shall be established
2 and set up on the books of the commonwealth a separate fund known as the Vietnam War
3 Commemoration Fund to commemorate the 50th anniversary of the United States’ withdrawal
4 from the Vietnam War, a conflict in which more than 1,300 service members from
5 Massachusetts lost their lives. The fund shall be established for the purposes of constructing and
6 renovating memorials, planning events, and generating educational materials. The fund shall be
7 administered by the Massachusetts Historical Commission, and municipalities may apply for
8 monies from the fund, subject to the approval of the commission. The fund shall be credited
9 with: (i) revenue from appropriations or other money authorized by the general court and
10 specifically designated to be credited to the fund; (ii) interest earned on such revenues; (iii) funds
1 of 2
11 from public and private sources such as gifts, grants, and donations to further commemoration of
12 the Vietnam War. Amounts credited to the fund shall not be subject to further appropriation.
13 (b) Amounts received from private sources shall be approved by the commission and
14 subject to review before being deposited in the fund to ensure that pledged funds are not
15 accompanied by conditions, explicit or implicit. The review shall be made publicly available.
2 of 2
