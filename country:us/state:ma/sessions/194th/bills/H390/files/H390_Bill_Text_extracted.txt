HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1095 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 390
By Representative Howitt of Seekonk, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 390) of
Steven S. Howitt relative to alcoholic beverage license suspensions. Consumer Protection and
Professional Licensure.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 322 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to license suspensions.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding the provisions of Chapter 138 of the General Laws or any other General
2 or Special law to the contrary, there shall be no distinction between a supermarket, convenience
3 store or package store whenever the holder of a license issued under the provisions of Section 15
4 of Chapter 138 is found to have been in violation of Section 34 of Chapter 138 relative to the
5 sale of alcohol to a minor or to an intoxicated individual that results in the suspension of all
6 commercial activity by the licensee as adjudicated by the local licensing authority in any city or
7 town which votes to authorize the granting of licenses for the sale of all alcoholic beverages. For
8 the purposes of this act, the suspension of commercial activity for a licensee shall mean that all
9 commercial activity, including the sale of non-alcoholic items, within the licensed establishment
10 shall cease for the period of the suspension.
