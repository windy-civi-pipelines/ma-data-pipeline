HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1213 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 394
By Representative Howitt of Seekonk, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 394) of
Steven S. Howitt relative to the displaying of motor fuel prices based on the method of payment.
Consumer Protection and Professional Licensure.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 325 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act establishing fuel price transparency.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 295C of Chapter 94 of the Massachusetts General Laws, as
2 appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after the first paragraph
3 the following paragraph:
4 If a retail dealer of motor fuel sells an identical grade of fuel at different prices per gallon
5 based on the customer’s method of payment, any sign displayed pursuant to the above paragraph
6 shall display each and every price per gallon for which a particular grade of fuel is sold and its
7 corresponding method of payment. Each price displayed on a sign for a particular grade of fuel
8 pursuant to this paragraph shall be the same size and presented in a substantially similar manner.
1 of 2
9 SECTION 2. Section 295D of Chapter 94 is hereby amended by inserting after the first
10 paragraph the following paragraph:
11 If a retail dealer of motor fuel sells an identical grade of fuel at different prices per gallon
12 based on the customer’s method of payment, any advertisement displayed pursuant to the above
13 paragraph shall display each and every price per gallon for which a particular grade of fuel is
14 sold and its corresponding method of payment. Each price advertised for a particular grade of
15 fuel pursuant to this paragraph shall be the same size and presented in a substantially similar
16 manner.
2 of 2
