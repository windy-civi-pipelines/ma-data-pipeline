HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 709 FILED ON: 1/11/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 4018
By Representative Nguyen of Andover, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 4018) of
Tram T. Nguyen relative to assault survivors. The Judiciary.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act protecting assault survivors.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 231 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after section 91B the
2 following section:-
3 Section 91C. (a) A person shall not, in an action for slander or libel, be held liable in
4 damages for any communication made, without malice, regarding an incident in which they were
5 the victim of sexual assault, harassment or discrimination.
6 (b) A prevailing defendant in any slander or libel action brought against that defendant
7 for making a communication to which subsection (a) applies shall be entitled to reasonable
8 attorney’s fees and costs, plus treble damages for any harm caused to them by the defamation
9 action against them, in addition to any other relief permitted by law.
10 (c) This section shall only apply to an individual who has, or at any time had, a
11 reasonable basis to file a complaint of sexual assault, harassment or discrimination, whether or
12 not the complaint was actually filed.
