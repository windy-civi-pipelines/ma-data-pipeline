HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 4239 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 4072
By Representative Arriaga of Chicopee, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 4072) of
Shirley B. Arriaga for legislation to exempt non-profits from certain motor vehicle registration
fees. Transportation.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to promote driving records for non profits free of charge.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 33 of chapter 90 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by adding the following subsection:-
3 (38) No fee shall be assessed or collected by the registrar or their authorized agent from a
4 nonprofit for a request search or copies of any driving records of the nonprofit’s employees and
5 volunteers.
