HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 4418 FILED ON: 2/12/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 4101
By Representative Garry of Dracut and Senator Kennedy, a joint petition (subject to Joint Rule
12) of Colleen M. Garry for legislation to authorize the Dracut Water Supply District to sell
water to certain communities. Environment and Natural Resources.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to the Dracut Water Supply District.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 The Dracut water supply district, established in section 1 of chapter 433 of the acts of
2 1905, may supply and sell water for domestic and other purposes to the town of Pelham, New
3 Hampshire or a water district servicing the town, the town of Hudson, New Hampshire or a water
4 district servicing the town and to the Kenwood water district of the town of Dracut.
