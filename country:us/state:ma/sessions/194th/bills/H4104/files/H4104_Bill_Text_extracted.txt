HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 4621 FILED ON: 5/1/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 4104
By Representative Shand of Newburyport, a petition (subject to Joint Rule 12) of Dawne Shand
for legislation to establish a sick leave bank for Daniel Yender, an employee of the
Massachusetts Department of Transportation. Public Service.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act establishing a sick leave bank for Daniel Yender, an employee of the Massachusetts
Department of Transportation.
Whereas, The deferred operation of this act would tend to defeat its purpose, which is to
establish forthwith a sick leave bank for a certain employee of the Massachusetts Department of
Transportation, therefore it is hereby declared to be an emergency law, necessary for the
immediate preservation of the public convenience.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, the Massachusetts
2 Department of Transportation shall establish a sick leave bank for Daniel Yender, an employee
3 of the Massachusetts Department of Transportation. Any employee of the Massachusetts
4 Department of Transportation may voluntarily contribute 1 or more sick, personal or vacation
5 days to the sick leave bank for use by Daniel Yender. If Daniel Yender terminates employment
6 with the Massachusetts Department of Transportation or requests to dissolve the sick leave bank,
7 any remaining time in the sick leave bank shall be transferred to the extended illness leave bank.
8 Sick leave bank days shall not be used for absences unrelated to the illness or disability that
1 of 2
9 necessitated the establishment of the sick leave bank as determined by the Massachusetts
10 Department of Transportation.
