By Representative LeBoeuf of Worcester, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 4136) of

(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to stabilization funds in the town of Leicester.

of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, Chapter 30 of the Acts of
2 2005 relative to the withdrawal of stabilization funds in the Town of Leicester is hereby
3 rescinded.
