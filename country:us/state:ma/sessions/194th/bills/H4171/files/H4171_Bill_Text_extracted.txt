HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 4743 FILED ON: 5/22/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 4171
By Representative Murray of Milford, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 4171) of
Brian W. Murray (by vote of the town) relative to vacancies among town meeting members in
the town of Milford. Election Laws. [Local Approval Received.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to any vacancy among town meeting members in Milford.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 271 of the acts of 1933 is hereby amended by striking out section 7,
2 as most recently amended by section 1 of chapter 297 of the acts of 1992, and inserting in place
3 thereof the following section:
4 Section 7. Any vacancy in the full number of town meeting members from any precinct,
5 whether arise from a failure of the registered voters thereof to elect, or from any other cause,
6 may be filled, until the next annual election, by the remaining members of the precinct from
7 among the registered voters thereof. Notice of any vacancy shall promptly be given by the Town
8 Clerk to the members from the precinct in which the vacancy or vacancies exist and he/she shall
9 call a special meeting of such members for the purpose of filling any vacancy. He/She shall
10 cause to be mailed to every such member a notice specifying the object, time and place of the
11 meeting with the notice of the time and place at which representative town meetings are to be
12 held as set forth in section three. At the said special meeting a majority of the members from
1 of 2
13 such precinct shall constitute a quorum, and they shall elect from their own numbers a chairman
14 and a clerk. The choice to fill any vacancy shall be by ballot and a majority of the votes cast shall
15 be required for a choice. The chairman and clerk shall count the ballots and shall make a
16 certificate of the choice and forthwith file the same with the Town Clerk, together with a written
17 acceptance by the member or members so chosen, who shall thereupon be deemed elected and
18 qualified as a town meeting member or members, subject to the right of all the town meeting
19 members to judge of the election and qualifications of the members as set forth in section three.
20 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
