HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 4820 FILED ON: 6/11/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 4235
By Representative Sylvia of Fairhaven and Senator Rodrigues, a joint petition (accompanied by
bill, House, No. 4235) of Mark D. Sylvia and Michael J. Rodrigues (by vote of the town) that the
town of Rochester be authorized to continue the employment of Scott Weigel as fire chief in said
town, notwithstanding the maximum age requirement. Public Service. [Local Approval
Received.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act authorizing the town of Rochester to continue the employment of fire chief Scott Weigel.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 1: Notwithstanding chapter 32 of the General Laws or any other general or
2 special law to the contrary, Scott Weigel, chief of the fire department of the town of Rochester,
3 may continue in service in such position until he reaches the age of 70; provided, however, that
4 he is mentally and physically capable of performing the duties of such position. The town of
5 Rochester may, at its own expense, require Scott Weigel to undergo an annual examination by an
6 impartial physician designated by the town to determine his capability to remain in service. No
7 further deductions shall be made from the regular compensation of Scott Weigel under said
8 chapter 32 for any service subsequent to his reaching 65 years of age and, upon retirement, Scott
9 Weigel shall receive a superannuation retirement allowance equal to that which he would have
10 been entitled had he retired at 65 years of age.
11 Section 2: This Act shall take effect upon its passage.
