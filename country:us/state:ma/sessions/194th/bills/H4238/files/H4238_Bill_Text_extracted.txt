HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 4807 FILED ON: 6/5/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 4238
By Representative Ayers of Quincy, a petition (subject to Joint Rule 12) of Bruce J. Ayers
relative to fraternal organizations conducting bazaars. Economic Development and Emerging
Technologies.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to fraternal organizations conducting bazaars.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 7A of chapter 271 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by striking out the last paragraph and inserting in place thereof the following
3 paragraph:-
4 No organization issued a permit under this section shall conduct more than 1 bazaar in
5 any single calendar day. The operation of a bazaar shall be limited to 5 consecutive hours.
