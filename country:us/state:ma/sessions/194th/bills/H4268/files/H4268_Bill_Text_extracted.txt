Court assembled:
The undersigned legislators and/or citizens respectfully petition for the adoption of the accompanying bill:
An Act to authorize the Wrentham Select Board to remove the position of lieutenant of the town
of Wrentham Police Department from the requirements of M.G.L. Ch. 31.
_______________
PETITION OF:

HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 4903 FILED ON: 7/9/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 4268
By Representative Vaughn of Wrentham, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 4268) of
Marcus S. Vaughn (by vote of the town) that the town of Wrentham be authorized to remove the
position of lieutenant of the police department of said town from the civil service law. Public
Service. [Local Approval Received.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to authorize the Wrentham Select Board to remove the position of lieutenant of the town
of Wrentham Police Department from the requirements of M.G.L. Ch. 31.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. The position of police lieutenant in the Town of Wrentham shall not be
2 subject to chapter 31 of the General Laws and related regulations applicable thereto.
3 SECTION 2. Section 1 of this act shall not impair the civil service status of any person
4 holding the position of police lieutenant in the Town of Wrentham on the effective date of this
5 act.
6 SECTION 3. This act shall take effect upon its passage."; and to authorize the General
7 Court to make clerical and editorial changes of form only to the bill, unless the Select Board
8 approves amendments to the bill before enactment by the General Court, and the Select Board
9 shall be authorized to approve amendments which shall be within the scope of the general public
10 objectives of the petition; and (b) to further authorize the Select Board to take any and all other
1 of 2
11 action necessary or advisable to remove the position of police lieutenant in the Town of
12 Wrentham from Civil Service
