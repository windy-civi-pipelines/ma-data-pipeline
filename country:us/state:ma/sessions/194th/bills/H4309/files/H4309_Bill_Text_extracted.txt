HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 4309
By Representative Ashe of Longmeadow and Senator Oliveira, a joint petition (accompanied by
bill, House, No. 4309) of Brian M. Ashe (by vote of the town) relative to preliminary elections in
the town of Longmeadow. Election Laws. [Local Approval Received.]

_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to preliminary elections in the town of Longmeadow.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 5 of the acts of 1988 is hereby repealed.
2 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
