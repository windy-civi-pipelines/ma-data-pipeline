The Commonwealth of Massachusetts

HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, July 30, 2025.
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 1668) of Kate Donaghue and Adrianne
Pusateri Ramos relative to support orders for adult children in need of
support, reports recommending that the accompanying bill (House, No.
4327) ought to pass.
For the committee,
MICHAEL S. DAY.
FILED ON: 7/21/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 4327
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to enforce support orders for adult children in need of support.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Subsection (b) of section 3 of chapter 188 of the General Laws, as
2 appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out, in line 22, the words
3 "or minor children;" and inserting in place thereof the following words:- ", minor or adult
4 children;".
