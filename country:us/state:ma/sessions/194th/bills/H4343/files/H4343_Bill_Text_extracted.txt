An Act ensuring access to full spectrum pregnancy care.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 17C of chapter 32A of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out, in the first paragraph, the words “or certified
3 nurse midwife;” and inserting in place thereof the following words:- “certified nurse midwife, or
4 licensed certified professional midwife;” and is further amended in the second paragraph by
5 striking out the following words “or certified nurse midwife” and inserting in place thereof the
6 following words:- “certified nurse midwife, or licensed certified professional midwife”.
7 SECTION 2. Said section 17C of chapter 32A as so appearing, is hereby further amended
8 in the third paragraph by striking out the following words “for abortion or abortion-related care”
9 as appearing in both instances.
10 SECTION 3. Said section 17C of chapter 32A, as so appearing, is hereby further
11 amended by inserting after the sixth paragraph the following paragraph:-
12 The commission shall ensure plan compliance with this section.
