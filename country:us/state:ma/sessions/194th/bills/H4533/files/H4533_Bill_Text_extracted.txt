HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 4533
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act expanding access to legal, regulated cannabis delivery.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Subsection (c) of section 4 of Chapter 94G of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out clauses (5) and (6) and inserting in place
3 thereof the following 3 clauses:-
4 (5) prohibit marijuana establishments from transferring or acquiring marijuana seeds,
5 clones, cuttings, plants or plant tissue from other marijuana establishments or from medical
6 marijuana treatment centers or prohibit a marijuana establishment from transferring or otherwise
7 selling marijuana to a marijuana retailer, a marijuana product manufacturer or a marijuana
8 cultivator;
9 (6) prohibit marijuana establishments from using inorganic cultivation methods; or
10 (7) prohibit an entity licensed to deliver marijuana or marijuana products from delivering
11 marijuana or marijuana products to any municipality in the commonwealth.
