HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 5208 FILED ON: 10/1/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 4582
By Representative Biele of Boston, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 4582) of David
Biele (with the approval of the mayor and city council) that city of Boston be authorized to
waive the maximum age requirement for firefighters for Stylianos Katikakis. Public Service.
[Local Approval Received.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act directing the City of Boston Fire Department to waive the maximum age requirement for
firefighters for Stylianos Katikakis.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1.
2 Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, the City of Boston Fire
3 Department shall waive the maximum age requirement for original appointment of firefighters
4 for Stylianos Katikakis. If Sylianos meets all other requirements set forth by the Boston Fire
5 Department, he shall be eligible to have his name certified for appointment to the position of
6 firefighter.
7 SECTION 2.
8 The provisions of this act are severable and if any provision, or portion thereof, should be
9 held to be unconstitutional or otherwise invalid by any court of competent jurisdiction, such
1 of 2
10 unconstitutionality or invalidity shall not affect the remaining provisions, which remain in full
11 force and effect.
12 SECTION 3.
13 This act shall take effect upon its passage.
2 of 2
