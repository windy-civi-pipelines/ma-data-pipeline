HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 5202 FILED ON: 9/29/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 4584
By Representative Ultrino of Malden, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 4584) of
Steven Ultrino (with the approval of the mayor and city council) relative to the appointment of
special police officers in the city of Malden. Public Service. [Local Approval Received.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act regarding the appointment of Special Police Officers in the City of Malden.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding section 1 of Chapter 31 of the General Laws or any other
2 general or special law to the contrary, including the provisions of Chapter 17 of the Acts of 2018,
3 the maximum age of eligibility for a retired Malden Police Officer to be appointed to the position
4 of Special Police Officers in the City of Malden shall be seventy (70) years of age.
5 SECTION 2. All other provisions of Chapter 17 of the Acts of 2018 shall remain in full
6 force and effect.
7 SECTION 3. This act shall take effect upon passage.
