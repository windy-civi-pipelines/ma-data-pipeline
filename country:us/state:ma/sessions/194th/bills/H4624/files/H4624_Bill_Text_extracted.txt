HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 5204 FILED ON: 9/30/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 4624
By Representatives Consalvo of Boston and Turco of Winthrop, a petition (subject to Joint Rule
12) of Rob Consalvo for legislation to establish a sick leave bank for Jeanne McCarron, an
employee of the Registry of Motor Vehicles. Public Service.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act establishing a sick leave bank for Jeanne McCarron.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, the registry of motor vehicles
2 shall establish a sick leave bank for Jeanne McCarron, an employee of the registry of motor
3 vehicles. Any employee of the registry of motor vehicles may voluntarily contribute 1 or more
4 sick, personal, or vacation days to the sick leave bank for use by Jeanne McCarron. If Jeanne
5 McCarron terminates employment with the registry of motor vehicles or requests to dissolve the
6 sick leave bank, any remaining time in the sick leave bank shall be transferred to the extended
7 illness leave bank. Sick leave bank days shall not be used for absences unrelated to the illness or
8 disability that necessitated the establishment of the sick leave bank as determined by the registry
9 of motor vehicles.
