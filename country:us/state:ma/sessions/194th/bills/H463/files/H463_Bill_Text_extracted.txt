HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 3843 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 463
By Representative Sabadosa of Northampton, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 463)
of Lindsay N. Sabadosa relative to warnings on pumps or other dispensing devices from which
motor fuel is sold. Consumer Protection and Professional Licensure.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to raise awareness of carbon dioxide emissions at gas stations.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 295C of Chapter 94, as so appearing, is hereby amended by
2 inserting after the first paragraph the following paragraph:-
3 A retailer dealer of motor fuel shall also publicly display and maintain on each pump or
4 other dispensing device from which motor fuel is sold, at least 1 warning sign, clearly visible,
5 stating “Warning! Every gallon of gasoline that is combusted dumps 19 pounds of carbon
6 dioxide into the atmosphere, which causes global heating.”
7 SECTION 2. Section 295C of said Chapter 94 of the General Laws, as so appearing, is
8 hereby amended by striking out the second paragraph and inserting in place thereof the following
9 paragraph:-
10 No sign, advertising material or other display or product that is placed upon, above or
11 around a pump or dispenser shall directly or indirectly obscure the posted price sign or warning
12 sign as required by the two preceding paragraphs.
