An Act relative to state grants targeting minority communities.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Subsection (p) of section 3 of Chapter 40G of the General Laws, as
2 appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended by adding the following:-
3 ; provided, further, that not less than 30 per cent of recipients be minority-owned
4 businesses or businesses with boards composed of not less than 50 per cent minority members.
5 SECTION 2. Subsection (29) of Section 3 of Chapter 23G, as so appearing, is hereby
6 amended by adding the following:-
7 ; provided, further, that not less than 30 per cent of recipients be minority-owned
8 businesses or businesses with boards composed of not less than 50 per cent minority members.
1 of 2
9 SECTION 3. Subsection (22) of Section 4 of Chapter 23I, as so appearing, is hereby
10 amended by adding the following:-
11 ; provided, further, that not less than 30 per cent of recipients be minority-owned
12 businesses or businesses with boards composed of not less than 50 per cent minority members.
13 SECTION 4. Subsection (t) of Section 3 of Chapter 40W, as so appearing, is hereby
14 amended by adding the following:-
15 ; provided, further, that not less than 30 per cent of recipients be minority-owned
16 businesses or businesses with boards composed of not less than 50 per cent minority members.
