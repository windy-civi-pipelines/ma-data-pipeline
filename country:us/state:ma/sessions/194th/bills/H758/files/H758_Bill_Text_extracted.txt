HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 889 FILED ON: 1/13/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 758
By Representative Chan of Quincy, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 758) of Tackey
Chan that the Division of Medical Assistance be authorized to include dentures, restorative,
endodontic and periodontal treatment within its covered services for certain persons ages 65 and
older. Elder Affairs.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 620 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to providing dental care to senior citizens.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 8 of the Chapter 118E of the General Laws, as appearing in the
2 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after the words “appropriate” in line 5 the
3 following:-
4 a1/4 “Dental Services, all dental services including dentures, restorative, endodontic, and
5 periodontal treatment.”
6 SECTION 2. Section 53 of Chapter 118E of the General Laws is hereby amended by
7 inserting at the end thereof the following paragraph: -
1 of 2
8 “The division shall include within its covered services for adults who are ages 65 and
9 older all federally optional services that were included in its state plan or demonstration program
10 in effect on January 1, 2002, including all dental services. Covered services for adults in the
11 MassHealth Essential program shall include dental services to the same extent as such services
12 were covered for adults in the MassHealth Basic program as of January 1, 2002.”
2 of 2
