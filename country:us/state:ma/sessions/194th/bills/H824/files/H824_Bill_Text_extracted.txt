HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2323 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 824
By Representative Donato of Medford, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 824) of Paul
J. Donato relative to political designations. Election Laws.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to political designations.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 1 of chapter 50 of the General Laws as appearing in the 2020 Official
2 Edition is hereby amended by striking out the word “fifty” in line 85 and putting in place thereof
3 the words: “five hundred”.
4 Section 2. Section 1 of Chapter 50 of the General Laws as appearing in the 2020 Official
5 Edition is hereby amended by inserting after the word “first.” in line 94 the words: “If, after the
6 expiration of two years from the date the political designation is approved, there are fewer than
7 one quarter of one percent of the total number of registered voters enrolled in the designation, it
8 shall be dissolved and any such voters enrolled shall be registered as “unenrolled.”
9 Section 3. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
