HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 4156 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 827
By Representative Frost of Auburn, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 827) of Paul K.
Frost relative to the voting procedures of presidential electors. Election Laws.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to the voting procedures for presidential electors.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 8 of chapter 53 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after “electors”, in line 16, the following
3 sentence:- One presidential elector shall be chosen from each congressional district, and two
4 presidential electors shall be chosen at-large.
5 SECTION 2. Chapter 54 of the General Laws, as so appearing, is hereby amended by
6 striking out section 148, and inserting in place thereof the following section:-
7 Section 148. The persons chosen as presidential electors shall meet at the state house on
8 the date fixed by federal law next following their election at three o’clock in the afternoon and
9 organize by the choice of a presiding officer and secretary. The state secretary shall call the
10 meeting to order, call the roll of electors, and preside until a presiding officer shall be chosen.
11 The secretary of the electors shall keep a journal of their proceedings and deposit the same in the
12 office of the state secretary, where it shall be recorded and filed. Each at-large presidential
1 of 2
13 elector shall cast his ballot for the presidential and vice-presidential candidates whose electors
14 received the highest number of votes in the state. Each congressional district presidential elector
15 shall cast his ballot for the presidential and vice-presidential candidates whose electors received
16 the highest number of votes in his congressional district.
