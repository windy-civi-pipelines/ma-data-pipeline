HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2217 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 987
By Representative LaNatra of Kingston, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 987) of
Kathleen R. LaNatra relative to the circular economy. Environment and Natural Resources.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to the circular economy.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 16 of the General Laws is hereby amended by adding, after section
2 21, the following section:-
3 Section 21A. The department shall update state waste system plans to reflect and
4 incorporate the following: the transition to a circular economy is a priority for the
5 Commonwealth of Massachusetts. Circular economy is defined as an economy that keeps
6 material, products, and services in circulation for as long as possible.
7 SECTION 2. Chapter 21 of the General Laws is hereby amended by adding, after section
8 67, the following section:-
9 Section 68. The Massachusetts Department of Environmental Protection shall create a
10 circular economy education and outreach program. This education and outreach program shall:
11 inform the public about what the circular economy is; inform the public on how they can
12 participate in the transition to a circular economy; inform Massachusetts businesses on what the
1 of 2
13 circular economy is; inform Massachusetts businesses on how to participate in the transition to a
14 circular economy.
