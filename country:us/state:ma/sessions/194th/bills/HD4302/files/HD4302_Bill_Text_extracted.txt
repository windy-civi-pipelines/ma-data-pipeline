In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to equality of chest baring.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Whereas gender discrimination is wrong,
2 And whereas the female body should not be stigmatized or fetishized,
3 Therefore MGL chapter 272 section 16 shall be amended to remove the words "female
4 breasts",
5 And equality of chest baring shall be the new law in Massachusetts.
