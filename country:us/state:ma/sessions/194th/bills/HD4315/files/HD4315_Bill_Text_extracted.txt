_______________
An Act relative to the appropriation of special education expense reimbursements and the
Education and Transportation Fund.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 2BBBBBB of Chapter 29 of the General Laws, as so appearing in
2 the most recent Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after subsection (b) the
3 following:- "In any fiscal year, no appropriation shall be made from the fund for any purpose
4 unless and until an appropriation has first been made to fully fund the special education
5 reimbursement program requirements under Chapter 71B, Section 5A, subsection (c), for the
6 same fiscal year.”
7 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect on July 1, 2025.
