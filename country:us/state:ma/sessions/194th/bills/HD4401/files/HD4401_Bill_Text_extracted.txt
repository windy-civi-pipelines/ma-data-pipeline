HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 4401 FILED ON: 2/12/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No.
By Representative Garry of Dracut, a petition (subject to Joint Rule 12) of Colleen M. Garry
relative to providing municipal notices to the public of hazardous waste sites. Environment and
Natural Resources.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 809 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act providing notice to the public of hazardous waste sites.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 19 of Chapter 21A shall be amended by inserting the following:
2 (c) A licensed hazardous waste site cleanup professional shall provide written notification
3 to the City or Town Clerk in the municipality where the site is located, by certified mail, within 7
4 days of the notification of the agency, federal or state requires posting of the known
5 contamination at the location. The Town or city clerk is required to maintain a database of all
6 locations of which they have received notification of contamination by property address and
7 make the database available to the public.
1 of 2
8 Failure to comply with this section shall result in a fine of $1000 for the first offense,
9 $2500 for any further offenses and up to 2 ½ years confinement in the house of correction or
10 both.
