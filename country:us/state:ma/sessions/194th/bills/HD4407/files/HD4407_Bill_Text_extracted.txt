HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 4407 FILED ON: 2/12/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No.
By Representative Garry of Dracut, a petition (subject to Joint Rule 12) of Colleen M. Garry for
legislation to establish an opt-out admissions lottery process for charter schools. Education.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 484 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to regulate school enrollment.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Subsection (n) of Section 89 of chapter 71 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2012
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out the first sentence and inserting in
3 place thereof
4 the following two sentences:-
5 Enrollment in a commonwealth charter school shall use an opt-out admissions lottery
6 process that automatically includes the names of all eligible students, without any required
7 application process for the school. A commonwealth charter school’s tuition payment shall be
8 adjusted to account for any students who cannot attend said school because necessary services
9 are not available.
