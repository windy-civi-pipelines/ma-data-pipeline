An Act relative to equity in the cannabis industry.

of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding chapter 94G of the General Laws as appearing in the 2020 Official
2 Edition, or any other general lor special law or regulation to the contrary, Host Community
3 Agreements entered into prior to August 11, 2022, shall not be reviewable or challenged if the
