HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 4593 FILED ON: 4/22/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No.
[Pin Slip]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act authorizing the city of Lowell to contract for the provision of transportation of homeless
students.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 1. Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, the City of Lowell
2 shall be exempted from General Law c. 30b when contracting for the transportation of homeless
3 students. The City of Lowell shall contract for said transportation of homeless students, whether
4 such transportation is entirely within the City of Lowell, outside the City of Lowell, or between
5 the City of Lowell and another municipality, in the same manner as it contracts for the
6 transportation of special education students, and upon such terms and conditions as the City
7 Manager shall determine to be in the best interest of the City.
8 Section 2. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
