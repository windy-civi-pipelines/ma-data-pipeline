HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 4619 FILED ON: 4/30/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No.
By Representative Howitt of Seekonk, a petition (subject to Joint Rule 12) of Steven S. Howitt
relative to motor vehicle license suspensions. Transportation.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to license suspensions.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 33 of Chapter 90, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended
2 by inserting after subsection 38 the following subsection:-
3 ‘(39) No fee for the reinstatement of a license following a period of suspension shall
4 exceed fifty dollars, if the sole reason for such a suspension was for a noncriminal traffic
5 violation.’
