Dear Committees on Ways and Means:
In accordance with Section 2E of Chapter 140 of the Acts of 2024, I am submitting a Fiscal Year
2025 transfer schedule for the Behavioral Health Outreach, Access and Support Trust Fund.
• 1595-4512 -- Behavioral Health Outreach, Access and Support Trust Fund, where the
transfer provides an operating subsidy from the General Fund to the Behavioral Health
Outreach, Access and Support Trust Fund for the purpose of promoting the awareness
and use of available behavioral health services.
Monthly transfer amounts are shown in the attachment.
I will notify you if any changes are made to the schedule. If you have any questions or concerns,
please contact me.
Sincerely,
William McNamara
Comptroller of the Commonwealth
