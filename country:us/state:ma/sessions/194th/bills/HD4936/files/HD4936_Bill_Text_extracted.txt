Court assembled:
The undersigned legislators and/or citizens respectfully petition for the adoption of the accompanying bill:

benefits to Dale R. Kiley.

NAME: DISTRICT/ADDRESS: DATE ADDED:
Donald R. Berthiaume, Jr. 5th Worcester 7/16/2025

HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 4936 FILED ON: 7/16/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No.
By Representative Berthiaume of Spencer and Senator Durant, a joint petition (subject to Joint
Rule 12) of Donald R. Berthiaume, Jr., and Peter J. Durant relative to the retirement benefits of
Dale R. Kiley, a retired state trooper. Public Service.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act authorizing the state board of retirement board to provide for the payment of retirement
benefits to Dale R. Kiley.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, the state board of retirement
2 shall remove Karen K. Kiley as the surviving spouse beneficiary of retired state trooper Dale R.
3 Kiley’s retirement pension benefits pursuant to option (c) of subdivision (2) of section 12 of
4 chapter 32 of the General Laws. The state board of retirement shall make Dale R. Kiley the sole
5 beneficiary of his retirement pension benefits, as provided for under option (a) of said
6 subdivision (2) of said section 12 of said chapter 32.
