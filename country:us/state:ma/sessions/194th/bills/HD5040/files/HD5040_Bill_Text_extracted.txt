HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No.
By Representative Cabral of New Bedford, a petition (subject to Joint Rule 12) of Antonio F. D.
Cabral relative to benefit payments by the New Bedford Police Association. Public Service.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to the New Bedford Police Association.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 451 of the acts of 2016 is hereby repealed.
2 SECTION 2. (a) Subject to the approval of the board of directors of the New Bedford
3 Police Association, and notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, the
4 association may pay:
5 (i) to beneficiaries named by a member of the association in good standing, a sum of
6 $24,000, upon the death of that member, if the deceased was an active member of the New
7 Bedford police department at the time of death;
8 (ii) to a member of the association in good standing, upon that member’s resignation
9 or retirement from the New Bedford police department, if that member has been pensioned from
10 the department due to an injury or disability, a sum equal to the greater of: (a) $150 for each year
11 of membership in the association for a period of not more than 20 years; or (b) $1,000;
1 of 2
12 (iii) to a member of the association in good standing, who has been a member for at
13 least 10 years or has paid membership dues to the association amounting to those membership
14 dues that would have been paid to the association during the immediately preceding 10 years
15 pursuant to the by-laws or who had been pensioned from the New Bedford police department for
16 an injury or other illness or disability, the greater of: (a) $3,750; or (b) $375 for each full year of
17 membership for a period of not more than 20 years, which amount shall reduce the benefit
18 otherwise payable pursuant to clauses (iv) and (v);
19 (iv) a sum equal to the greater of : (a) $1,000; or (b) $150 for each year of
20 membership in the association for a period of not more than 20 years, to a member upon that
21 member’s resignation or retirement from the New Bedford police department, if that person was
22 a member of the association in good standing and a member of the association for not less than
23 10 years;
24 (v) a sum of $12,000, less the sum of money paid pursuant to clauses (ii) or (iii), to
25 any beneficiaries named by a member of the association upon that member's death if that
26 member was a member of the association in good standing and a retired member of the New
27 Bedford police department; and
28 (b) A sum equal to $3,000 shall be paid to a member of the association in good
29 standing upon the death of the spouse of that member, subject to a vote of approval of the board
30 of directors of the association.
2 of 2
