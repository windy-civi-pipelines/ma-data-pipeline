HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 5213 FILED ON: 10/6/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No.
By Representative Markey of Dartmouth, a petition (subject to Joint Rule 12) of Christopher M.
Markey for legislation to establish a cost-of-living adjustment for disabled veteran annuities.
Veterans and Federal Affairs.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act establishing a cost-of-living adjustment for the disabled veteran annuity.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 6B of chapter 115 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2024
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting, in the first paragraph, in line 12, after the word
3 “1”, the following:
4 “The payment shall be subject to an annual cost-of-living-adjustment valued at the same
5 percentage as the annual Social Security and Supplemental Security Income cost-of-living
6 adjustment, as determined by the United States Social Security Administration”.
7 SECTION 2. Section 6B of chapter 115 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2024
8 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting, in the second paragraph, in line 3, after the
9 word “1”, the following:
10 “The payment shall be subject to an annual cost-of-living-adjustment valued at the same
11 percentage as the annual Social Security and Supplemental Security Income cost-of-living
12 adjustment, as determined by the United States Social Security Administration”.
1 of 2
13 SECTION 3. Section 6B of chapter 115 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2024
14 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting, in the third paragraph, in line 3, after the word
15 “1”, the following:
16 “The payment shall be subject to an annual cost-of-living-adjustment valued at the same
17 percentage as the annual Social Security and Supplemental Security Income cost-of-living
18 adjustment, as determined by the United States Social Security Administration”.
2 of 2
