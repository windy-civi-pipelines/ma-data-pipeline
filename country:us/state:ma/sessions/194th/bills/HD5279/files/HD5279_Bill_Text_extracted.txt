[Pin Slip]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act authorizing the town of Truro to regulate the use of pesticides.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding chapter 132B of the General Laws, or any other general or
2 special law to the contrary, the town of Truro is hereby authorized to enact a bylaw regulating
3 the storage, use and application of pesticides within said town of Truro, to establish civil
4 penalties not to exceed $1,000 for each offense, and to otherwise enforce said bylaw through any
5 lawful means.
6 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
