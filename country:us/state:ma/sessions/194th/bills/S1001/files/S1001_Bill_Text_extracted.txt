SENATE DOCKET, NO. 1484 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1001
By Mr. Kennedy, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1001) of Edward J. Kennedy,
Vanna Howard, Rodney M. Elliott and Tara T. Hong for legislation to increase housing
production in the city of Lowell. Housing.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 2977 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to increase housing production in the city of Lowell.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 2 of chapter 193 of the acts of 2000, as appearing in section 1 of
2 chapter 97 of the acts of 2002, is hereby amended by striking out, each time it appears, the figure
3 “45” and inserting in place thereof, in each instance, the following figure:- 40.
4 SECTION 2. Section 3 of said chapter 193, as so appearing, is hereby amended by
5 striking out the figure “180” and inserting in place thereof the following figure:- 187.
6 SECTION 3. Said section 3 of said chapter 193, as so appearing, is hereby further
7 amended by striking out the figure “81” and inserting in place thereof the following figure:- 75.
