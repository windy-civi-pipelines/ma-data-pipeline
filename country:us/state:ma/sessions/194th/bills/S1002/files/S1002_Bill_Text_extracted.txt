SENATE DOCKET, NO. 1582 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1002
By Mr. Kennedy, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1002) of Edward J. Kennedy for
legislation to require a municipal vote relative to tenant occupancy of accessory dwelling units
on smaller lots. Housing.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to accessory dwelling units on smaller lots.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 7 of Chapter 150 of the Acts of 2024 is hereby amended by adding after the word
2 "rental" the following words:-
3 In zoning districts designated for single family housing and where the minimum lot size
4 is 10,000 square feet or less, accessory dwelling units by right will be limited to owner occupied
5 properties and accessory dwelling units can only be occupied by members of the property
6 owner's extended family. The owner occupancy and family relation requirements may be waived
7 with a vote of the municipality.
