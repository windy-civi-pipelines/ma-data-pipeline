SENATE DOCKET, NO. 2232 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1101
By Mr. Feeney, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1101) of Paul R. Feeney for
legislation relative to personal injury protection payments. The Judiciary.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to personal injury protection payments.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 34M of Chapter 90 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after the word “payable”, in line 82 the
3 following words:- whether in whole or in part,
4 SECTION 2. Section 34M of Chapter 90 of the General Laws, as so appearing, is hereby
5 amended by inserting after the word “fees”, in line 94, the following words:- except the court
6 shall not assess attorney’s fees or costs if, within thirty days of service of the summons and
7 complaint, the insurer tenders to the Plaintiff the amount due and payable.
