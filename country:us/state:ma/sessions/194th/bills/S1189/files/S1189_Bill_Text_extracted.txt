SENATE DOCKET, NO. 1329 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1189
By Mr. Montigny, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1189) of Mark C. Montigny for
legislation to address leaving the scene of an accident resulting in death aka Katie Brienzo's Law.
The Judiciary.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 1066 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to address leaving the scene of an accident resulting in death aka Katie Brienzo's Law.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. This bill may be known as an Act to update the definition leaving the scene
2 of an accident a/k/a Katie’s Law.
3 SECTION 2. Section 24 of chapter 90 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
4 edition, is hereby amended by striking out, in lines 968 through 970, the following words:- “or
5 by imprisonment in a jail or house of correction for not less than one year nor more than two and
6 one-half years and by a fine of not less than one thousand dollars nor more than five thousand
7 dollars”.
1 of 2
8 SECTION 3. Section 24 of chapter 90 of the General Laws, as so appearing, is hereby
9 amended by striking, in line 972, the words “one year” and inserting in place thereof the words
10 “two and one-half years”.
11 SECTION 4. Section 24 of chapter 90 of the General Laws, as so appearing, is hereby
12 amended by striking, in lines 975, the words “one year” and inserting in place thereof the words
13 “two and one-half years”.
2 of 2
