SENATE DOCKET, NO. 1583 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1196
By Mr. Montigny, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1196) of Mark C. Montigny and
Christopher Hendricks for legislation to prevent child labor exploitation and trafficking by
increasing fines and penalties. The Judiciary.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to prevent child labor exploitation and trafficking.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 1 of chapter 149 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after the words “tenement house workrooms,”
3 the following words:- seafood packing or processing facilities,
4 SECTION 2. Section 56 of said chapter 149 is hereby amended by inserting after the
5 words “any hospital in a nonprofessional capacity,” the following words:- any seafood packing
6 or processing facility,
7 SECTION 3. Said section 56 is hereby amended by striking the following words:- but,
8 except in the work of fish processing when necessary in the judgment of the attorney general,
9 and then only during the months of June, July, August, September and October, shall not exceed
10 fifty-two
1 of 3
11 SECTION 4. Section 60 of said chapter 149 is hereby amended by inserting after the
12 words “or mechanical establishment,” the following words:- , or in any seafood packing or
13 processing facility.
14 SECTION 5. Section 62 of said chapter 149 is hereby amended by inserting after the
15 words "(16) in any job or occupation requiring the possession or use of a firearm," the following
16 words:- (17) or in any seafood packing or processing facility.
17 SECTION 6. Section 78 of said chapter 149 is hereby amended by striking the figure
18 “$500” and inserting in place thereof the following figure:- $20,000
19 SECTION 7. Said section 78 of chapter 149 is hereby amended by striking the figure
20 “$5,000” and inserting in place thereof the following figure:- $100,000
21 SECTION 8. Said section 78 of chapter 149 is hereby amended by inserting at the end
22 thereof the following sentence:- The penalty provided for in this section shall be in addition to
23 and may be imposed concurrently with any other remedy or penalty provided for in this chapter.
24 SECTION 9. Section 78A of said chapter 149 is hereby amended in subsection (a) by
25 striking the figures “$250,” “$500,” and “$2,500,” respectively, and inserting in place thereof the
26 following figures:- “$20,000,” “$50,000,” and “$100,000,”.
27 SECTION 10. Said section 78A of chapter 149 is hereby amended by inserting at the end
28 thereof the following subsection:- (e) The penalty provided for in this section shall be in addition
29 to and may be imposed concurrently with any other remedy or penalty provided for in this
30 chapter.
2 of 3
31 SECTION 11. Section 79 of said chapter 149 is hereby amended by striking the words
32 “twenty-five nor more than two hundred dollars or by imprisonment for not more than two
33 months, or both” and inserting in place thereof the following words:- $20,000 nor more than
34 $100,000 or by imprisonment for not more than 6 months, or both
3 of 3
