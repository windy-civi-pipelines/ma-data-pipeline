SENATE DOCKET, NO. 89 FILED ON: 1/7/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1201
By Mr. Moore, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1201) of Michael O. Moore for
legislation to maintain protective orders after plaintiff's death. The Judiciary.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 1070 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act maintaining protective orders.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 3 of chapter 209A of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by striking subsection (h) and inserting in place thereof the following
3 subsection:-
4 (h) ordering the defendant to refrain from abusing or contacting the plaintiff’s child, or
5 child in plaintiff’s care or custody, unless authorized by the court, which shall survive the death
6 of the plaintiff until such order expires or is vacated by the court;.
