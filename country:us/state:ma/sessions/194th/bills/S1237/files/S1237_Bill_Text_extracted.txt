SENATE DOCKET, NO. 2428 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1237
By Mr. Oliveira, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1237) of Jacob R. Oliveira for
legislation to prohibit gambling on political outcomes. The Judiciary.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to wagering on political outcomes.
Whereas, The deferred operation of this act would tend to defeat its purpose, which is to
to prohibit gambling on political outcomes in the commonwealth, therefore it is hereby declared
to be an emergency law, necessary for the immediate preservation of the public convenience.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 271 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby
2 amended by inserting after section 6C the following section:-
3 Section 6D. For the purposes of this section, "political outcomes" shall include, but not
4 be limited to, elections for public office within the commonwealth, appointments to public
5 office, the passage, amendment or failure of legislative measures and any other official decision-
6 making processes conducted by political bodies within the commonwealth. No person shall
7 knowingly place, accept or engage in any form of betting or wagering on political outcomes. No
8 person or entity shall establish, maintain, operate or promote any platform, service or venue,
9 whether physical or digital, for the purpose of facilitating betting or wagering on political
10 outcomes. This section shall not apply to lawful political contributions, campaign financing or
11 other activities expressly permitted under chapter 55.
