Court assembled:
The undersigned legislators and/or citizens respectfully petition for the adoption of the accompanying bill:
An Act to add sex and gender as protected classes for crime prosecutions.

PETITION OF:
NAME: DISTRICT/ADDRESS:
Rebecca L. Rausch Norfolk, Worcester and Middlesex
William N. Brownsberger Suffolk and Middlesex

SENATE DOCKET, NO. 2123 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1246
By Ms. Rausch, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1246) of Rebecca L. Rausch and
William N. Brownsberger for legislation relative to the addition of sex and gender as a protected
class category for crime prosecutions. The Judiciary.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 914 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to add sex and gender as protected classes for crime prosecutions.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 39 of chapter 265 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended in subsection (a) by inserting in line 4, after the words “national origin” the
3 following words:- , sex, gender.
