SENATE DOCKET, NO. 672 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1292
By Ms. Comerford, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1292) of Joanne M. Comerford
for legislation relative to state employment postings. Labor and Workforce Development.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 1150 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to state employment postings.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 4J of chapter 7 of the General Laws is hereby amended by adding the following
2 paragraph:-
3 Every agency and institution of the commonwealth shall transmit to the human resources
4 division all employment opportunities, notices and postings, excluding positions subject to
5 section 46D of chapter 30, chapter 31 and chapter 150E, including, but not limited to, notices of
6 job opportunities, new positions and vacancies. The division shall immediately place all such
7 information in the statewide employment computerized referral system. No position shall be
8 filled without full compliance with this paragraph.
