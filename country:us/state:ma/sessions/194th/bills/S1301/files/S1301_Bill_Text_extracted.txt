SENATE DOCKET, NO. 1817 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1301
By Mr. DiDomenico, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1301) of Sal N. DiDomenico
for legislation to protect an employee's right to rebuttal of personnel records. Labor and
Workforce Development.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 1156 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act protecting an employee's right to rebuttal of personnel records.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION1. Section 148A of Chapter 149 of the Massachusetts General Laws, as
2 appearing in the 2022 official edition, is hereby amended by inserting at the end of the first
3 sentence the following:- “or under the personnel records provisions in Section 52C of this
4 chapter.”
5 SECTION 2. Section 150 of said Chapter 149 of the Massachusetts General Laws, as
6 appearing in the 2022 official edition, is hereby amended in the second paragraph by inserting
7 after “33E,” the following:- “52C,”
