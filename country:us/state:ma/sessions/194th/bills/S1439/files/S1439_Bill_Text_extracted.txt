SENATE DOCKET, NO. 2157 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1439
By Ms. Dooner, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1439) of Kelly A. Dooner, David F.
DeCoste, David F. DeCoste, Colleen M. Garry and others for legislation to exempt certain
adjacent communities without direct access to MBTA services from zoning laws. Municipalities
and Regional Government.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act exempting certain adjacent communities from MBTA zoning requirements.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 3A of Chapter 40A of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official
2 Edition, is hereby amended by adding the following subsection:-
3 (d) Notwithstanding any general or special law, regulation, or policy to the contrary, the
4 requirements of this section shall not apply to any municipality categorized as an “Adjacent
5 Community” or an “Adjacent Small Town” without direct access to Massachusetts Bay
6 Transportation Authority service.
