SENATE DOCKET, NO. 1741 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 147
By Ms. Kennedy, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 147) of Robyn K. Kennedy for
legislation to replace stolen benefits relative to electronic benefit transfer fraud. Children,
Families and Persons with Disabilities.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to replace stolen benefits.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Subsection (B) of Section 2 of chapter 18 of the General Laws, as so appearing in the
2 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended after line 172 by inserting the following new
3 paragraph:-
4 (t). promptly replace the full amount of financial assistance or nutrition benefits stolen
5 through electronic benefit transfer card skimming, card cloning or other fraudulent methods,
6 including organized identity theft schemes, to the extent the federal government fails to provide
7 federal funds to replace stolen benefits, provided that the thefts have been reported to or
8 identified by the Department in accordance with procedures for making and verifying
9 replacement claims established by the department.
