SENATE DOCKET, NO. 1318 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1520
By Mr. Durant, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1520) of Peter J. Durant, Susan E.
Chester , and Bruce E. Tarr for legislation to protect a patient’s right to a support person at
health care facilities. Public Health.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to protect a patient’s right to a support person at health care facilities.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 70E of chapter 111 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official
2 Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after subsection (o) the following subsection:-
3 (p) to a person of their choosing to serve as a support person. The support person may be
4 present with the patient 24 hours a day in patient-accessible areas of the health care facility or
5 provider’s office to accompany the patient while the patient is receiving inpatient or outpatient
6 treatment or is consulting with their health care provider. A health care facility may temporarily
7 restrict the presence of the support person during surgeries or emergency interventions. Health
8 care facilities shall not require the support person to receive any vaccination as a condition for
9 access to the patient.
