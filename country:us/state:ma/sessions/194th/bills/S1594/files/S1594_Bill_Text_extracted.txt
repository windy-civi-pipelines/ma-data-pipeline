SENATE DOCKET, NO. 335 FILED ON: 1/11/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1594
By Mr. O'Connor, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1594) of Patrick M. O'Connor for
legislation to protect patients with Alzheimer's, dementia, and other psychiatric illnesses. Public
Health.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 1438 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to protect patients with Alzheimer's, dementia, and other psychiatric illnesses.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 9 of chapter 19D of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2014
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after clause (18)(b) the following clause: (19) in
3 the case of a resident suffering from Alzheimer's, dementia, or other psychiatric illnesses,
4 facilities are prohibited from transferring patients off-premises for medication adjustments
5 without the approval of the patient, guardian, or person legally responsible for the patient.
6 SECTION 2. Section 70E of chapter 111 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2014
7 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after clause (o) the following clause: (p)in the
8 case of a resident suffering from Alzheimer's, dementia, or other psychiatric illnesses, facilities
9 are prohibited from transferring patients off-premises for medication adjustments without the
10 approval of the patient, guardian, or person legally responsible for the patient.
