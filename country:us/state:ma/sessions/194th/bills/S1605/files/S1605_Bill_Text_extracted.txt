SENATE DOCKET, NO. 2128 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1605
By Mr. O'Connor, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1605) of Patrick M. O'Connor for
legislation to establish statewide food truck regulations. Public Health.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 2670 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act establishing statewide food truck regulations.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 111 of the General laws is hereby amended by adding the
2 following section:-
3 Section 244. The department of public health shall promulgate regulations for the annual
4 health inspection of food trucks. The department shall prescribe rules and regulations relative to
5 inspection schedules, documentation of inspections, standards for acceptable cleanliness and the
6 costs of such inspections.
7 SECTION 2. The department of public health shall promulgate the regulations set forth in
8 section 1 not later than 1 year after the enactment of this act.
