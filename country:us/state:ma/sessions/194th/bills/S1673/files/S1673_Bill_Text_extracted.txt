SENATE DOCKET, NO. 434 FILED ON: 1/13/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1673
By Mr. Durant, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1673) of Peter J. Durant for
legislation relative to live firearm training requirements. Public Safety and Homeland Security.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to live fire training requirements.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 74 of chapter 135 of the Acts of 2024 is hereby repealed.
2 SECTION 2. Section 152 of chapter 135 of the Acts of 2024 is hereby repealed.
