SENATE DOCKET, NO. 1471 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1679
By Mr. Durant, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1679) of Peter J. Durant for
legislation to protect personal data in ammunition transactions. Public Safety and Homeland
Security.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to protecting personal data in ammunition transactions.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 37 of Chapter 135 of the Acts of 2024, is hereby amended by
2 deleting the word “ammunition” in subsection (h).
3 SECTION 2. Section 37 of Chapter 135 of the Acts of 2024, is hereby amended by
4 deleting the words “and ammunition” in subsection (h)(i).
5 SECTION 3. Section 37 of Chapter 135 of the Acts of 2024, is hereby amended by
6 deleting the words “or ammunition” in subsection (h)(ii).
7 SECTION 4. Section 37 of Chapter 135 of the Acts of 2024, is hereby amended by
8 deleting the words “or ammunition” in subsection (h)(iii).
9 SECTION 5. Section 37 of Chapter 135 of the Acts of 2024, is hereby amended by
10 deleting the words “or ammunition” in subsection (h)(iii).
1 of 2
11 SECTION 6. Section 37 of Chapter 135 of the Acts of 2024, is hereby amended by
12 deleting the words “or ammunition” in subsection (k).
2 of 2
