SENATE DOCKET, NO. 2585 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1695
By Mr. Fattman, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1695) of Ryan C. Fattman for
legislation to restore certain civil rights pertaining to firearms. Public Safety and Homeland
Security.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act restoring certain civil rights.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 41 of Chapter 135 of the Acts of 2024, is hereby amended by
2 deleting subsection (a) in its entirety.
3 SECTION 2. Section 41 of Chapter 135 of the Acts of 2024, is hereby amended by
4 deleting in subsection (b) the following:- “provided, however, that for transfers and purchases of
5 firearms that are not rifles and shotguns that are not large capacity or semiautomatic, the
6 transferee shall also have a valid permit to purchase under section 131A.”
7 SECTION 3. Section 41 of Chapter 135 of the Acts of 2024, is hereby amended by
8 deleting in subsection (c) the following:- "(C) a person with a firearm identification card under
9 section 129B; provided, however, that for transfers and purchases of firearms that are not rifles
10 and shotguns that are not large capacity or semi-automatic, the transferee shall have a valid
11 permit to purchase under section 131A."
1 of 2
12 SECTION 4. Section 41 of Chapter 135 of the Acts of 2024, is hereby amended by
13 deleting subsection (e) in its entirety.
