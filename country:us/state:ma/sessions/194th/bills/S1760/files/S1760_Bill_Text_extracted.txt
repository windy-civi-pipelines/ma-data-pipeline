SENATE DOCKET, NO. 565 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1760
By Mr. Rush, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1760) of Michael F. Rush for
legislation relative to arson investigators. Public Safety and Homeland Security.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 1585 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to arson investigators.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 94B of Chapter 32 of the General Laws, as so appearing in the 2022 Official
2 Edition, is hereby amended in subsection 1 in line 14 by inserting after the words "military
3 reservation fire department" the following:- "members of municipal police departments who
4 perform arson investigations."
