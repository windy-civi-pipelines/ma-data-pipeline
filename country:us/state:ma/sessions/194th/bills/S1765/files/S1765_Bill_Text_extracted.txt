SENATE DOCKET, NO. 47 FILED ON: 1/7/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1765
By Mr. Tarr, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1765) of Bruce E. Tarr for legislation
relative to the visibility of fire hydrants. Public Safety and Homeland Security.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 1594 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to the visibility of fire hydrants.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 148 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after section 27B the
2 following section:-
3 Section 27C. Fire hydrant visibility
4 Fire hydrants installed in the commonwealth or any political subdivision thereof located
5 in any public or private way shall be painted in colors that maximize their visibility, contrast
6 with their surroundings, and do not include the color white unless used as a secondary color to
7 contrast with the surroundings of the area.
