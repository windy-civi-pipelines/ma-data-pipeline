SENATE DOCKET, NO. 696 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1774
By Mr. Tarr, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1774) of Bruce E. Tarr for legislation
relative to black powder for hunters and re-enactors. Public Safety and Homeland Security.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to black powder for hunters and re-enactors.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 45 of Chapter 135 of the Acts of 2024 as appearing is hereby amended by
2 inserting at the end of subsection (a) the following:-- "(iv) a resident or nonresident for the
3 carrying or possession of ammunition for an antique firearm as defined in section 121 of Chapter
4 140 including black powder, or a black powder substitutes, and related ammunition
5 components."
