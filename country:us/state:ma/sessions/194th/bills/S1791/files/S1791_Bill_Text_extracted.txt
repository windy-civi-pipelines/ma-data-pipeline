Michael D. Brady

Court assembled:
The undersigned legislators and/or citizens respectfully petition for the adoption of the accompanying bill:
An Act relative to local public housing authorities access to the dental and vision insurance plan
managed by the group insurance commission.

PETITION OF:
NAME: DISTRICT/ADDRESS:
Michael D. Brady Second Plymouth and Norfolk
Danillo A. Sena 37th Middlesex 4/3/2025

SENATE DOCKET, NO. 281 FILED ON: 1/10/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1791
By Mr. Brady, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1791) of Michael D. Brady for
legislation relative to local public housing authorities access to the dental and vision insurance
plan managed by the group insurance commission. Public Service.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 1611 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to local public housing authorities access to the dental and vision insurance plan
managed by the group insurance commission.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding Chapter 32A of the General Laws, the Group Insurance Commission
2 shall provide dental and vision insurance benefits to employees of a housing or redevelopment
3 authority upon a vote by its governing body.
