By Mr. Brady, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1795) of Michael D. Brady for
legislation relative to creating employment opportunities for veterans. Public Service.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to veteran inclusion.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 26 of Chapter 31 of the General Laws as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition is
2 hereby amended by striking out in line 7 "(4) all others," and inserting the words " (4) member
3 of the Massachusetts National Guard or Reserves of the United States Armed Forces with no less
4 than two years of continuous service; (5) all others,"
