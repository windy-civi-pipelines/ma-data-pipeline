SENATE DOCKET, NO. 2161 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1813
By Mr. Crighton, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1813) of Brendan P. Crighton for
legislation relative to underperforming pension systems. Public Service.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 1627 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to the funding ratio of the public employee retirement system.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Sub-section (c1/2) of sub-section (8) of section (22) of Chapter 32 is
2 amended by deleting the third sentence and inserting the following:
3 Any system found by the commission to have a funded ratio of less than fifty percent and
4 an annual rate of return during the previous ten years that is at least three percentage points less
5 than that of the PRIT Fund rate of return over the same period shall be declared underperforming
6 by the commission.
7 SECTION 2. The final sentence of a sub-section (c 1/2) of sub-section (8) of section 22of
8 Chapter 32 is amended by deleting in the final sentence the words: "Shall be in perpetuity." and
9 inserting: "May not be revoked for five years."
