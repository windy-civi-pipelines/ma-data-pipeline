NAME: DISTRICT/ADDRESS:
John J. Cronin Worcester and Middlesex
James C. Arena-DeRosa 8th Middlesex 10/6/2025

SENATE DOCKET, NO. 995 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1815
By Mr. Cronin, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1815) of John J. Cronin for
legislation relative to GIC healthcare coverage for group 4 members pending disability
retirement. Public Service.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 1634 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to GIC healthcare coverage for group 4 members pending disability retirement.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 28N of chapter 32 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2018 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by inserting the following paragraph:-
3 (b) When any Group 4 member has applied and/or going through the accidental or
4 ordinary disability retirement process said member's GIC health care benefits will not be
5 terminated and be continued until the members retirement is approved.
