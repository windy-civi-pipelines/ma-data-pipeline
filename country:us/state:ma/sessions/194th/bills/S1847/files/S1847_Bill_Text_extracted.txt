SENATE DOCKET, NO. 2070 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1847
By Mr. Feeney, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1847) of Paul R. Feeney for
legislation to provide diabetes prevention program benefits. Public Service.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 1661 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to provide diabetes prevention program benefits for GIC insurees.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 32A of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby
2 amended by inserting after section 17R the following section:-
3 Section 17S. The commission shall provide to any active or retired employee of the
4 commonwealth who is insured under the group insurance commission and is identified as
5 someone with prediabetes those items and services furnished under a diabetes prevention
6 program recognized as a National Diabetes Prevention Program as established by the federal
7 Centers for Disease Control and Prevention.
