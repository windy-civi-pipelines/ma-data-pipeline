SENATE DOCKET, NO. 2090 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1848
By Mr. Feeney, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1848) of Paul R. Feeney for
legislation to protect local retirees. Public Service.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 1662 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to protecting local retirees.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 9E of Chapter 32B of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by adding the following sentence:-
3 In the event that the governmental unit approves an increase in the percent of the
4 premium to be contributed by its retired employees, the percent of premium being paid by a
5 retired employee at that time will not be increased to the higher percent.
6 SECTION 2. The provisions of this act shall take effect on January 1, 2026.
