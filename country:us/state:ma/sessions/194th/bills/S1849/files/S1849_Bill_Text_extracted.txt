legislation relative to court officers injured on duty. Public Service.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 1663 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to court officers injured on duty.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 The last paragraph of section 58 of chapter 30 of the General Laws, as appearing in the
2 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended by adding the following sentence:-
3 All amounts payable under this paragraph shall be paid at the same times and in the same
4 manner as, and for all purposes shall be deemed to be, the regular compensation of such court
5 officer.
