SENATE DOCKET, NO. 121 FILED ON: 1/8/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1885
By Mr. Moore, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1885) of Michael O. Moore for
legislation relative to the timely and consistent payment of law enforcement personnel. Public
Service.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 1705 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to the timely and consistent payment of law enforcement personnel.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 39M of chapter 30 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official
2 Edition, is hereby amended by adding the following paragraph:-
3 (f) In order to ensure the timely and consistent payment for police details assigned to
4 public works projects performed under this chapter, the project awarding authority shall assume
5 direct responsibility for maintaining police detail records, receiving invoices from, and making
6 direct payments to those entities providing such paid details. The cost of police details shall not
7 be included as part of the bid submitted by any offerer responding to a bid solicitation conducted
8 in accordance with this chapter. This paragraph shall be referenced or included as part of the bid
9 documents for public works projects.
