SENATE DOCKET, NO. 403 FILED ON: 1/13/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1888
By Mr. Moore, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1888) of Michael O. Moore for
legislation relative to the retirement of MassPort officers. Public Service.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 1704 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to the retirement of MassPort officers.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 3 of chapter 32 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is
2 amended in line by inserting after the word “men”, the second time it appears, in line 295, the
3 following words:-
4 “; employees of the Massachusetts Port Authority who are employed as port officers and
5 supervisors of said employees who shall include port sergeants, lieutenants, captains, assistant
6 chiefs, deputy chiefs and directors”.
