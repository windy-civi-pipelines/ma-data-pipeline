SENATE DOCKET, NO. 592 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1906
By Mr. Rush, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1906) of Michael F. Rush for
legislation to include certain certified emergency telecommunicators in Group 2 of the
contributory retirement system for public employees. Public Service.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 1730 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to Massachusetts certified emergency telecommunicators.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 32, Section 3, paragraph G, “Group 2” of the Massachusetts General Laws is
2 hereby amended by inserting in line 283 after the words “Cushing hospital” the following:-
3 "; and all full time Dispatchers, Dispatch Supervisors, Communications Center Directors
4 or Communications Chiefs certified by the Commonwealth of Massachusetts State 911
5 Department as Certified Telecommunicators as defined by MA 560 CMR 5.04".
