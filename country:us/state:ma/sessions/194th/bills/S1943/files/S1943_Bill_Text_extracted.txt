SENATE DOCKET, NO. 1280 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1943
By Mr. Crighton, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1943) of Brendan P. Crighton for
legislation relative to rolling stock. Revenue.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 1778 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to rolling stock.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 1 of chapter 64H of the general laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting, after the definition of “Retail establishment”,
3 the following definition:-
4 “Rolling stock”, trucks, tractors, and trailers, used by common carriers to transport goods
5 in interstate commerce.
6 SECTION 2. Section 6 of chapter 64H, as so appearing, is hereby amended by inserting,
7 after subsection (xx), the following new subsection:-
8 (yy) Sales of rolling stock.
1 of 2
9 SECTION 3. Section 1 of chapter 64I, as so appearing, is amended by inserting in line 5,
10 after the words “retail establishment”, the following new words:- , “rolling stock”.
11 SECTION 4. Section 7 of chapter 64I, as so appearing, is hereby amended by inserting,
12 after subsection (e), the following new subsection:-
13 (f) Storage, use, or other consumption of rolling stock.
2 of 2
