SENATE DOCKET, NO. 883 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1985
By Mr. Fattman, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1985) of Ryan C. Fattman and
Bruce E. Tarr for legislation relative to 100% disabled veteran excise tax exemptions. Revenue.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 1811 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to 100% disabled veteran excise tax exemptions.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 1 of chapter 60A of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by inserting, in line 99, after the word “has” the following words:- “been
3 awarded permanent disability compensation by the United States Department of Veterans Affairs
4 at a 100% rating, or".
