SENATE DOCKET, NO. 392 FILED ON: 1/13/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2052
By Mr. O'Connor, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 2052) of Patrick M. O'Connor for
legislation to establish a local option to exempt fishermen from property taxes. Revenue.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 1905 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act establishing a local option to exempt fishermen from property taxes.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 5 of chapter 59 of the general laws, as appearing in the 2008 Official Edition, is
2 hereby amended by inserting at the end thereof the following new section:-
3 Fifty-eighth. Upon the acceptance of this section by a city or town, the board of assessors
4 may grant real and personal property tax abatement up to 100 per cent of the total tax assessed to
5 a resident who is a licensed commercial fisherman, lobsterman, oyster farmer, or other type of
6 fish farmer, subject to eligibility criteria to be established by the board of assessors.
1 of 1
