SENATE DOCKET, NO. 406 FILED ON: 1/13/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2056
By Mr. O'Connor, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 2056) of Patrick M. O'Connor for
legislation relative to relieving minors of income tax obligations. Revenue.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 1920 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to relieving minors of income tax obligations.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 4 of Chapter 62 of the general laws, as so appearing, is hereby amended by
2 striking the first sentence and replacing it with the following:-
3 “Residents over the age of 18 shall be taxed on their taxable income, and non-residents
4 over the age of 18 shall be taxed to the extent specified in section 5A on their taxable income, as
5 follows”.
