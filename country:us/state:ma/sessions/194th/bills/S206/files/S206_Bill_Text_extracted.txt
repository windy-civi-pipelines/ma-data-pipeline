SENATE DOCKET, NO. 1039 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 206
By Mr. Cronin, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 206) of John J. Cronin for
legislation relative to a model carrier reporting requirement on delivery of wines, malt beverages
or distilled spirits. Consumer Protection and Professional Licensure.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 4697 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to a model carrier reporting requirement on delivery of wines, malt beverages or
distilled spirits.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 138 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after section 18C the
2 following section:-
3 Section 18D. It shall be the duty of every railroad company, express company, common
4 carrier, contract carrier, firm or corporation that brings, carries or transports wines, malt
5 beverages or distilled spirits into the commonwealth to prepare, and file monthly with the
6 commission, a report which shall include the name and address of the supplier, the weight of
7 wine, malt beverages or distilled spirits; provided, however, said reporting requirement shall not
8 apply to deliveries from certificate of compliance holders licensed pursuant to section 18B to
9 wholesalers licensed pursuant to section 18.
1 of 2
10 Reports shall be submitted to the commission, on a monthly basis, on forms prescribed by
11 the commission.
2 of 2
