SENATE DOCKET, NO. 43 FILED ON: 1/7/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2074
By Mr. Tarr, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 2074) of Bruce E. Tarr for legislation
relative to the taxation of historic property. Revenue.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 1934 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to the curatorship program.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 44 of chapter 85 of the acts of 1994 is hereby amended by inserting at the end
2 thereof the following sentence:-
3 Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, any historic property
4 designated as such by this section shall not be subject to the provisions of chapter 59 of the
5 General Laws.
