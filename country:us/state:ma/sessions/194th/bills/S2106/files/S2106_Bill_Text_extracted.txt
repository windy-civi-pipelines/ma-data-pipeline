The Commonwealth of Massachusetts

William N. Brownsberger

Court assembled:
The undersigned legislators and/or citizens respectfully petition for the adoption of the accompanying bill:
An Act relative to removing social security numbers from death certificates.
_______________
PETITION OF:
NAME: DISTRICT/ADDRESS:
William N. Brownsberger Suffolk and Middlesex
1 of 1
SENATE DOCKET, NO. 34 FILED ON: 1/6/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2106
By Mr. Brownsberger, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 2106) of William N.
Brownsberger for legislation to remove social security numbers from death certificates. State
Administration and Regulatory Oversight.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to removing social security numbers from death certificates.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 1 of chapter 46 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is
2 hereby amended by striking the words: “social security number,”;
3 and further in the fourth paragraph of said section by inserting after the first sentence the
4 following sentence:- The record of death shall not include the social security number of the
5 deceased.
1 of 1
