SENATE DOCKET, NO. 229 FILED ON: 1/9/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2128
By Mr. Durant, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 2128) of Peter J. Durant and Donald
R. Berthiaume, Jr. for legislation to authorize the commissioner of capital asset management and
maintenance to convey certain land in the town of Brookfield. State Administration and
Regulatory Oversight.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 2991 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act authorizing the commissioner of capital asset management and maintenance to convey
certain land in the town of Brookfield.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. (a) Notwithstanding sections 32 to 37, inclusive, of chapter 7C of the
2 General Laws or any other general or special law to the contrary, the commissioner of capital
3 asset management and maintenance, in consultation with the department of the state police, may
4 convey to Mary Faugno-Wiles a certain parcel of land in the town of Brookfield. The parcel
5 consists of 35,000 square feet, more or less, and is shown on a plan of land entitled “Plan of
6 Land at 14 Mill Street”, dated March 23, 2021, prepared by Summit Engineering & Land
7 Survey, Inc. and described in a deed recorded in the Worcester district registry of deeds in book
8 2514, page 139. Such plan of land shall be placed on file with the division of capital asset
9 management and maintenance. The conveyance shall be subject to such additional conditions
1 of 3
10 and restrictions as the commissioner of capital asset management and maintenance, in
11 consultation with the department of state police, may prescribe.
12 (b) As consideration for the conveyance of the parcel of land described in subsection (a),
13 Mary Faugno-Wiles shall convey to the commissioner of capital asset management and
14 maintenance a certain parcel of land on South Maple street in the town of Brookfield consisting
15 of 38,000 square feet, more or less, and shown on the plan of land described in subsection (a)
16 and described in a deed recorded in the Worcester district registry of deeds in book 21493, page
17 111 or such other land or interest in land as the commissioner of capital asset management and
18 maintenance, in consultation with the department of the state police, may deem appropriate.
19 (c) As further consideration for the conveyance in subsection (a), Mary Faugno-Wiles
20 shall pay the difference between the full and fair market value of the parcel described in
21 subsection (a) and the full and fair market value of the parcel described in subsection (b). The
22 commonwealth shall not be obligated to pay any consideration to Mary Faugno-Wiles if the full
23 and fair market value of the parcel of land described in subsection (b) exceeds the full and fair
24 market value of the parcel described in subsection (a). The full and fair market value of the
25 parcels shall be determined by the commissioner of capital asset management and maintenance
26 on the basis of independent appraisals commissioned by the commissioner.
27 (d) The commissioner of capital asset management and maintenance shall submit the
28 appraisal or appraisals to the inspector general for review and approval, which shall include an
29 examination of the methodology utilized for the appraisal or appraisals. The inspector general
30 shall file the report with the commissioner of capital asset management and maintenance who
31 shall submit copies of the appraisals and the inspector general’s review and approval to the house
2 of 3
32 and senate committees on ways and means and the joint committee on state administration and
33 regulatory oversight not less than 15 days prior to the execution of documents effecting the
34 transfers described in section 1.
35 (e) Any consideration paid by Mary Faugno-Wiles to the commonwealth for the
36 conveyance authorized by this act shall be deposited into the General Fund.
37 SECTION 2. Mary Faugno-Wiles, or any successors or assigns, shall be responsible for
38 all costs that the commissioner of capital asset management and maintenance may deem
39 necessary to complete the conveyances authorized by section 1 including, but not limited to, the
40 costs of all appraisals, surveys, deeds and other document preparation, recording or filing fees
41 and any other expenses.
