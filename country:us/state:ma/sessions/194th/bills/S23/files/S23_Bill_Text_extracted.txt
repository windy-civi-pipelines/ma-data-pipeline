SENATE DOCKET, NO. 2641 FILED ON: 2/4/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 23
By Mr. Crighton, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 23) of Brendan P. Crighton (by a
vote of the town) to enable the appointment of alternates to the Swampscott Conservation
Commission. Municipalities and Regional Government. [Local Approval Received.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act enabling the appointment of alternates to the Swampscott Conservation Commission.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. (a) Notwithstanding Section 8C of Chapter 40 of the General Laws, the
2 Select Board may appoint up to 2 alternate members to the Conservation Commission for terms
3 not to exceed 3 years.
4 (b) The Chair of the Conservation Commission may designate an alternate member to sit
5 on the Commission in the case of absence, inability to act, or conflict of interest on the part of a
6 member of the Commission, or in the event of a vacancy on the Commission, until the vacancy is
7 filled, or take any action relative thereto.
