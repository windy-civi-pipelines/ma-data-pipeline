SENATE DOCKET, NO. 161 FILED ON: 1/9/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2321
By Mr. Tarr, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 2321) of Bruce E. Tarr and Rodney M.
Elliott for legislation relative to renewable energy production technologies.
Telecommunications, Utilities and Energy.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 2558 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to renewable energy production technologies.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Subsection (l) of section 139 of chapter 164 of the General Laws, inserted
2 by section 55 of chapter 179 of the acts of 2022, is hereby amended by striking out after the
3 words “other,” the words:- such solar.
4 SECTION 2. Said subsection (l) of said section 139 of said chapter 164, as so inserted, is
5 hereby further amended by inserting after the word “each,” each time it appears, the following
6 word:- solar.
