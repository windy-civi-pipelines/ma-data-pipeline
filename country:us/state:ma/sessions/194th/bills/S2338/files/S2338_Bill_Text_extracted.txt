SENATE DOCKET, NO. 1135 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2338
By Mr. Rush, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 2338) of Michael F. Rush for
legislation to name the Roslindale Branch of the Boston Public Library as the McGrory-
Roslindale Branch of the Boston Public Library. Tourism, Arts and Cultural Development.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act honoring Mary McGrory.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 19E of Chapter 78 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after
2 subsection (1) the following:-
3 (a) the Roslindale Branch of the Boston Public Library shall be named the "McGrory-
4 Roslindale Branch of the Boston Public Library" to honor Mary McGrory, a resident of
5 Roslindale and a Pulitzer Prize winning journalist".
