SENATE DOCKET, NO. 1348 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2363
By Mr. Cronin, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 2363) of John J. Cronin for
legislation to establish a separate fund to be known as the InterCity Regional Passenger Rail
Fund. Transportation.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act fostering intercity passenger rail service.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 29 of the General Laws shall be amended by adding the following
2 section:-
3 2EEEEEE. There shall be established and set up on the books of the commonwealth a
4 separate fund to be known as the InterCity Regional Passenger Rail Fund, which shall be used
5 exclusively for financing rail transportation-related projects to expand and support
6 passenger/commuter rail throughout the Commonwealth, beginning with the Route 2 Corridor.
7 The fund shall be subject to appropriation and shall be used for transportation related expenses of
8 the executive office of transportation or any successor agency or authority to pay for contracts,
9 services and debt service on bonds issued by, the Commonwealth for InterCity Passenger Rail
10 expansion or improvements. Funds may also be expended for contract assistance for providing
11 such services.
1 of 3
12 SECTION 2. Chapter 23K of the General Laws shall be amended by adding the
13 following section:-
14 Section 59A. There shall be established and set up on the books of the commonwealth a
15 New Gaming Revenue Fund which shall receive twenty-five percent of the gross gaming
16 revenues collected from the Region D gaming licensee. The commission shall be the trustee of
17 the fund and shall transfer monies in the fund as follows:
18 (1) 100 per cent of the revenue received from a category 1 licensee shall be transferred to
19 the InterCity Passenger Rail Fund established pursuant to section 2EEEEEE of chapter 29;
20 (2) new gaming tax revenue from other licensed gaming operations as deemed by the
21 legislature.
22 SECTION 3. Subsection (a) of section 19 of chapter 23K of the general laws is hereby
23 amended by striking out clause (1) and inserting in place thereof the following clause (1):
24 “(1) region A: suffolk, middlesex, essex and norfolk counties;”
25 SECTION 4. Said subsection (a) of said section 19 of said chapter 23K, as so appearing,
26 is hereby further amended by striking out, at the end of clause (2), the word “and”.
27 SECTION 5. Said subsection (a) of said section 19 of said chapter 23K, as so appearing,
28 is hereby further amended by striking out, at the end of clause (3), the word “counties“ and
29 inserting in place thereof the following words:-
30 “counties; and”
2 of 3
31 SECTION 6. Said subsection (a) of said section 19 of said chapter 23K, as so appearing,
32 is hereby further amended by inserting after clause (3) the following clause (4):
33 “(4) region D: consisting of the cities and towns located in the Worcester Northern
34 District Registry of Deeds, being Ashburnham, Fitchburg, Leominster, Lunenburg, and
35 Westminster.”
36 SECTION 7. Said section 19 of said chapter 23K, as so appearing, is hereby further
37 amended by inserting a new subsection (h), which shall provide as follows:
38 “(h) No gaming license shall be issued by the commission in region D unless it shall be
39 situated on a site containing not less than 70 contiguous acres.”
3 of 3
