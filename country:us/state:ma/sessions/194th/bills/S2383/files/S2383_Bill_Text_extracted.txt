SENATE DOCKET, NO. 410 FILED ON: 1/13/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2383
By Mr. Durant, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 2383) of Peter J. Durant and
Kimberly N. Ferguson for legislation to designate a certain bridge in the town of Princeton as the
Captain Christopher J. Sullivan memorial bridge. Transportation.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act designating a certain bridge in the town of Princeton as the Captain Christopher J.
Sullivan memorial bridge.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. The bridge on Route 140 in East Princeton, over the Keyes Brook, bridge
2 number P16017, in the town of Princeton, shall be designated and known as the "Captain
3 Christopher J. Sullivan Memorial Bridge.”
4 SECTION 2. The Massachusetts Department of Transportation shall erect and maintain a
5 suitable marker bearing the designation in compliance with the standards of the department.
6 SECTION 3. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
