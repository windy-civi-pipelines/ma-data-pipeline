Court assembled:
The undersigned legislators and/or citizens respectfully petition for the adoption of the accompanying bill:
An Act relative to the air medal license plate.

NAME: DISTRICT/ADDRESS:
Jason M. Lewis Fifth Middlesex

SENATE DOCKET, NO. 1737 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2408
By Mr. Lewis, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 2408) of Jason M. Lewis and Jacob
R. Oliveira for legislation to create the air medal license plate. Transportation.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to the air medal license plate.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 2 of chapter 90 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is
2 hereby amended by inserting after the term “Distinguished Flying Cross” in lines 505, 511, 513
3 and 539, the following words:- Air Medal
