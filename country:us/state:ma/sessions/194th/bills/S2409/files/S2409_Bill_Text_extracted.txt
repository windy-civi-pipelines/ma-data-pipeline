SENATE DOCKET, NO. 1440 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2409
By Ms. Lovely, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 2409) of Joan B. Lovely for
legislation relative to permitting fees for operating multiple vehicles under a single farm plate
registration. Transportation.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 2241 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to ensure financial viability in the transportation of farm goods.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 30A of Chapter 85 of the General Laws is hereby amended by adding to
2 subsection (5), prior to the last sentence of that paragraph, the following words:-
3 “However, a farmer or person involved in the harvesting of forest products as defined by
4 the regulations of the registry of motor vehicles, who is operating multiple vehicles under a
5 single farm plate registration under the provisions of MGL Chapter 90 Section 5, shall be
6 required to pay a single fee which shall cover permits for all vehicles operating under said
7 registration. The fee shall be based on that vehicle operating under this registration, with the
8 greatest difference between the weight for which the permit is issued and the weight for which
9 the vehicle is registered.”
