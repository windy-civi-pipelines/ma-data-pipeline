SENATE DOCKET, NO. 2591 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 241
By Mr. Feeney, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 241) of Paul R. Feeney and Adam J.
Scanlon for legislation relative to problem gambling and the public health trust fund. Consumer
Protection and Professional Licensure.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to problem gambling and the public health trust fund.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 58 of chapter 23K of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by adding the following sentences:-
3 The Department of Public Health shall make annual reports to the Speaker of the House,
4 the President of the Senate, the Joint Committee on Public Health, the Joint Committee on
5 Economic Development and Emerging Technologies on its treatment programs funded by the
6 fund, including but not limited to: (i) the treatment programs funded by the Fund and the
7 amounts they receive; (ii) the number of individuals served by each treatment program funded by
8 the Fund, including whether they are new clients or continuing clients in the programs; (iii)
9 demographic information of those served by treatment programs funded by the fund, including
10 but not limited to the variables of sex, age, race, ethnicity, income, education, geography; and
11 (iv) the outcomes obtained for individuals enrolled in treatment programs funded by the Fund.
