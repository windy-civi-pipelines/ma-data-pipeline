SENATE DOCKET, NO. 576 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2465
By Mr. Fattman, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 2465) of Ryan C. Fattman and
Bruce E. Tarr for legislation to expand the National Guard welcome home veterans' bonus to all
deployed members. Veterans and Federal Affairs.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 2320 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act expanding the National Guard welcome home veterans' bonus to all deployed members.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 78 of chapter 10 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by inserting, in line 23, after the word “service” the following words:- or if
3 called to active service as a current member of the Massachusetts National Guard or an Armed
4 Forces Reserve Component of a Massachusetts based unit.
1 of 1
