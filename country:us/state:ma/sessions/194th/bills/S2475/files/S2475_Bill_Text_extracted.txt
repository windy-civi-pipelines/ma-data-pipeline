SENATE DOCKET, NO. 1299 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2475
By Mr. Lewis (by request), a petition (accompanied by resolve, Senate, No. 2475) of Vincent
Dixon, for legislation to memorialize the Massachusetts Delegation, and the United States
Congress, on the need for general support of NATO, and public education, regarding this subject.
Veterans and Federal Affairs.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
Resolve for memorializing the Massachusetts Delegation, and the United States Congress, on the
need for general support of NATO, and public education, regarding this subject.
1 Resolved, 1.) The Great and General Court of Massachusetts, hereby Resolves that we
2 generally support, the long-established membership of The United States, as a member state, of
3 NATO, the North Atlantic Treaty Organization. Since its founding in 1949, it has grown, helped
4 to secure the peace of the North Atlantic, and much of Europe, and expanded freedom across
5 many countries.
6 2.) Many of our NATO allies, have fought together with us, in recent, and ongoing
7 conflicts, and have provided significant assistance to us, in our ongoing war against terrorism.
8 3.) It is in the interests of keeping peace, and expanding freedom, that The United States
9 remain active, and supportive of NATO, and Massachusetts wishes to go on record, as
10 supporting those efforts.
1 of 2
11 4.) Massachusetts as a leader, in The United States, and The United States as a leader in
12 The North Atlantic Community, would benefit, by both strong support, and significant public
13 education, on the values, and benefits of this membership, and its role in our lives.
2 of 2
