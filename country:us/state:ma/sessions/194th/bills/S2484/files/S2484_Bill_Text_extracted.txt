SENATE DOCKET, NO. 520 FILED ON: 1/13/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2484
By Mr. Rush, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 2484) of Michael F. Rush and Paul
McMurtry for legislation relative to protecting veterans. Veterans and Federal Affairs.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 2340 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to protecting veterans.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 98A of chapter 272 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after each instance of the words “physically
3 handicapped persons” appearing lines 3, 10, and 15 the following words:- or veteran.
4 SECTION 2. The first paragraph of section 92A of chapter 272, as appearing in section 1,
5 chapter 134 of the acts of 2016, is hereby amended by inserting after the word “disability” the
6 following words:- or veteran status.
7 SECTION 3. Section 98 of chapter 272 of the General Laws, as appearing in section 3,
8 chapter 134 of the acts of 2016, is hereby amended by inserting after the word “ancestry” the
9 following words:- or veteran status.
