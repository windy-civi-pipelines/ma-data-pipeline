SENATE DOCKET, NO. 597 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2490
By Mr. Rush, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 2490) of Michael F. Rush and Paul
McMurtry for legislation relative to outreach to veterans. Veterans and Federal Affairs.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 2349 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to outreach to veterans.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 115, Section 5 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is
2 hereby amended by inserting after paragraph 7, the following:-
3 "The Secretary of Veterans’ Services shall promulgate regulations to reimburse
4 municipalities for outreach costs by local departments of veterans’ Services to be administered
5 by veterans’ service officers veterans’ service officers of 75% of all outreach expenses not to
6 exceed $2,000".
