By Mr. Velis, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 2502) of John C. Velis for legislation
to increase annuity for disabled Veterans and Gold Star families. Veterans and Federal Affairs.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 2356 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act ensuring annuity eligibility for Gold Star Spouses.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 6B of chapter 115 of the General Laws, as so appearing, is hereby further
2 amended by striking out, in paragraph 3, the words “, provided, that the surviving spouse does
3 not remarry,”.
