The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_________________
PRESENTED BY:
Patrick M. O'Connor and Patrick Joseph Kearney
_________________
Court assembled:
The undersigned legislators and/or citizens respectfully petition for the adoption of the accompanying bill:
An Act relative to parking fines in the town of Scituate.
_______________
PETITION OF:
NAME: DISTRICT/ADDRESS:
Patrick M. O'Connor First Plymouth and Norfolk
Patrick Joseph Kearney 4th Plymouth 7/31/2025
1 of 1
SENATE DOCKET, NO. 3106 FILED ON: 7/23/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2577
By Mr. O'Connor and Mr. Kearney of Scituate a joint petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No.
2577) of Patrick M. O'Connor (by vote of the town) relative to parking fines in the town of
Scituate. Municipalities and Regional Government. [Local Approval Received.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to parking fines in the town of Scituate.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 385 of the acts of 1991 is hereby amended by the text “not exceed
2 fifty dollars” and replacing the same with the following:- “not exceed one hundred and fifty
3 dollars”.
4 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
5 Provided that the General Court may reasonably vary the form and substance of the
6 requested legislation with the scope of the general public objectives of this petition; or take any
7 other action relative thereto.
