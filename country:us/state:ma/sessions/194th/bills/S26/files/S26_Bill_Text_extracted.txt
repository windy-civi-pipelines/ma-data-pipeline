The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_________________
PRESENTED BY:
Cynthia Stone Creem
_________________
Court assembled:
The undersigned legislators and/or citizens respectfully petition for the adoption of the accompanying bill:
An Act authorizing the town of Brookline to prohibit or restrict the application of second-
generation anticoagulant rodenticides.
_______________
PETITION OF:
NAME: DISTRICT/ADDRESS:
Cynthia Stone Creem Norfolk and Middlesex
William F. MacGregor 10th Suffolk 1/24/2025
Tommy Vitolo 15th Norfolk 2/26/2025
1 of 1
SENATE DOCKET, NO. 2613 FILED ON: 1/21/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 26
By Ms. Creem, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 26) of Cynthia Stone Creem,
William F. MacGregor and Tommy Vitolo (by vote of the town) for legislation to authorize the
town of Brookline to prohibit or restrict the application of second-generation anticoagulant
rodenticides. Environment and Natural Resources. [Local Approval Received.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act authorizing the town of Brookline to prohibit or restrict the application of second-
generation anticoagulant rodenticides.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding Chapter 132B of the General Laws or any other general or
2 special law to the contrary, the town of Brookline may by bylaw prohibit or restrict the
3 application of second generation anticoagulant rodenticides within the town of Brookline,
4 including the application of such pesticides by licensed commercial applicators as defined in 333
5 C.M.R. 10.00.
6 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
