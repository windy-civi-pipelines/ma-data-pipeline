SENATE DOCKET, NO. 2739 FILED ON: 3/7/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2629
By Ms. Friedman, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 2629) (subject to Joint Rule 12)
of Cindy F. Friedman for legislation to adjust the MBTA assessment. Transportation.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act adjusting the MBTA assessment.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 2 of chapter 6C of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out, in lines 21 to 24, the words "1 of the
3 fourteen cities and towns, as defined in said section 1 of said chapter 161A; 1 of whom shall
4 have municipal government experience in 1 of the 51 cities and towns" and inserting in place
5 thereof the following words:- 1 of the 13 cities and towns, as defined in said section 1 of said
6 chapter 161A; 1 of whom shall have municipal government experience in 1 of the 52 cities and
7 towns.
8 SECTION 2. Section 1A of chapter 40A of the General Laws, as so appearing, is hereby
9 amended by striking out, in lines 32 to 34, the words "(i) one of the 51 cities and towns as
10 defined in section 1 of chapter 161A; (ii) one of the 14 cities and towns as defined in said section
11 1 of said chapter 161A;" and inserting in place thereof the following words:- (i) one of the 52
12 cities and towns as defined in section 1 of chapter 161A; (ii) one of the 13 cities and towns as
13 defined in said section 1 of said chapter 161A;.
1 of 3
14 SECTION 3. Section 147 of chapter 160 of the General Laws, as so, is hereby amended
15 by striking out, in lines 22 and 23, the words "the fourteen cities and towns or the sixty-four
16 cities and towns" and inserting in place thereof the following words:- the 13 cities and towns or
17 the 52 cities and towns.
18 SECTION 4. Section 1 of chapter 161A of the General Laws, as so, is hereby amended
19 by striking out the definition of ''51 cities and towns'' and inserting in place thereof the following
20 definition:- ''52 cities and towns'', the cities and towns of Arlington, Bedford, Beverly, Braintree,
21 Burlington, Canton, Cohasset, Concord, Danvers, Dedham, Dover, Framingham, Hamilton,
22 Hingham, Holbrook, Hull, Lexington, Lincoln, Lynn, Lynnfield, Manchester-by-the-Sea,
23 Marblehead, Medfield, Melrose, Middleton, Nahant, Natick, Needham, Norfolk, Norwood,
24 Peabody, Quincy, Randolph, Reading, Salem, Saugus, Sharon, Stoneham, Swampscott,
25 Topsfield, Wakefield, Walpole, Waltham, Wellesley, Wenham, Weston, Westwood, Weymouth,
26 Wilmington, Winchester, Winthrop and Woburn.
27 SECTION 5. Said section 1 of said chapter 161A, as so appearing, is hereby further
28 amended by striking out the definition of ''Fourteen cities and towns'' and inserting in place
29 thereof the following definition:- "13 cities and towns", the cities and towns of Belmont, Boston,
30 Brookline, Cambridge, Chelsea, Everett, Malden, Medford, Milton, Newton, Revere, Somerville
31 and Watertown.
32 SECTION 6. Section 7 of said chapter 161A, as so appearing, is hereby amended by
33 striking out, in lines 17 and 18, the words "the 14 cities and towns; (ii) the 51 cities and towns"
34 and inserting in place thereof the following words:- the 13 cities and towns; (ii) the 52 cities and
35 towns.
2 of 3
36 SECTION 7. Section 7A of said chapter 161A, as so appearing, is hereby amended by
37 striking out the words “14 cities and towns”, each time they appear, and inserting in place
38 thereof, in each instance, the following words:- 13 cities and towns.
39 SECTION 8. Said section 7A of said chapter 161A, as so appearing, is hereby further
40 amended by striking out the words “51 cities and towns”, each time they appear, and inserting in
41 place thereof, in each instance, the following words:- 52 cities and towns.
42 SECTION 9. Section 9 of said chapter 161A, as so appearing, is hereby amended by
43 striking out the words “14 cities and towns”, each time they appear, and inserting in place
44 thereof, in each instance, the following words:- 13 cities and towns.
45 SECTION 10. Said section 9 of said chapter 161A, as so appearing, is hereby further
46 amended by striking out the words “51 cities and towns”, each time they appear, and inserting in
47 place thereof, in each instance, the following words:- 52 cities and towns.
3 of 3
