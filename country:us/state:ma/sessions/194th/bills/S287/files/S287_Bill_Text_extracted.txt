The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_________________
PRESENTED BY:
Bruce E. Tarr

Court assembled:
The undersigned legislators and/or citizens respectfully petition for the adoption of the accompanying bill:
An Act relative to horse riding instructors.
_______________
PETITION OF:
NAME: DISTRICT/ADDRESS:
Bruce E. Tarr First Essex and Middlesex
James C. Arena-DeRosa 8th Middlesex 3/7/2025
Joan B. Lovely Second Essex 3/18/2025
David F. DeCoste 5th Plymouth 3/27/2025

SENATE DOCKET, NO. 42 FILED ON: 1/7/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 287
By Mr. Tarr, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 287) of Bruce E. Tarr and James C.
Arena-DeRosa for legislation relative to horse riding instructors licensing. Consumer Protection
and Professional Licensure.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 216 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to horse riding instructors.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 2A of chapter 128 of the general laws is hereby repealed.
