SENATE DOCKET, NO. 693 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 288
By Mr. Tarr, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 288) of Bruce E. Tarr for legislation
relative to bonded warehouses. Consumer Protection and Professional Licensure.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to bonded warehouses.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Massachusetts General Laws Section 129D of Chapter 140 as amended by Section 46(d)
2 of Chapter 135 of the Acts of 2024 is hereby amended by striking the following:-- "pursuant to
3 subsection (c)".
