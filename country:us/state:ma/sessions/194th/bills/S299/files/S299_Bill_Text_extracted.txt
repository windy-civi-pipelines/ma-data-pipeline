SENATE DOCKET, NO. 2401 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 299
By Mr. Feeney, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 299) of Paul R. Feeney for
legislation relative to economic development and infrastructure in the Stadium Area of Route1.
Economic Development and Emerging Technologies.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to economic development and infrastructure in the Stadium Area of Route 1.
Whereas, The deferred operation of this act would tend to defeat its purpose, which is to
revise forthwith the parking fee requirement in the towns of Sharon, Walpole and Wrentham,
therefore it is hereby declared to be an emergency law, necessary for the immediate preservation
of the public convenience.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 7 of chapter 16 of the acts of 1999 is hereby amended by inserting
2 after paragraph (1) the following paragraph:-
3 (1 ½) There shall be a regional economic development fund to be known as the Route 1
4 Stadium Area Economic Development and Infrastructure Fund, to be administered by the town
5 of Foxborough for the purposes of promoting economic development and infrastructure
6 improvements along the Route 1 corridor and adjacent neighborhoods in the towns of Sharon,
7 Walpole, Wrentham and Foxborough. There shall be credited to the fund: (i) any funds that may
8 be appropriated or transferred for deposit into the fund; (ii) any revenue derived from the parking
9 fees collected pursuant to paragraph (2); and (iii) all other amounts credited or transferred into
1 of 2
10 the fund from any other source; provided, however, that amounts credited to the fund shall not be
11 subject to appropriation. Money remaining in the fund at the end of a fiscal year shall not revert
12 to the General Fund and shall be available for expenditure in subsequent fiscal years.
13 SECTION 2. Paragraph (2) of said section 7 of said chapter 16 of the acts of 1999 is
14 hereby amended by striking out the words “returned to the commonwealth for deposit in the
15 general fund” and inserting in place thereof the following words:- transferred to the Route 1
16 Stadium Area Economic Development and Infrastructure Fund established under paragraph
17 (1½).
18 SECTION 3. Said paragraph (2) of said section 7 of said chapter 16 of the acts of 1999 is
19 hereby further amended by adding the following sentence:- Notwithstanding section 8 of this act
20 or any other general or special law to the contrary, the annual aggregate amount of the
21 administrative parking fees to be collected pursuant to this paragraph shall be reduced in
22 accordance with the following schedule: by 20 per cent in 2027; by 40 per cent in 2028; by 60
23 per cent in 2029; by 80 per cent in 2030; and by 100 per cent in 2031.
