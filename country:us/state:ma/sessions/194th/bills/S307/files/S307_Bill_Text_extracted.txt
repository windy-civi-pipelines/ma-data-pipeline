SENATE DOCKET, NO. 460 FILED ON: 1/13/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 307
By Mr. O'Connor, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 307) of Patrick M. O'Connor for
legislation relative to LLC filing fees. Economic Development and Emerging Technologies.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 236 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to LLC filing fees.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 12 of Chapter 156C of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2014 Official
2 Edition, is hereby amended by striking subsection (d) and inserting in place thereof the
3 following:-
4 (d) The fee for the filing of the certificate of organization required by subsection (a) shall
5 be five hundred dollars. The fee for the filing of the annual report required by subsection (c) shall
6 be five hundred dollars, except as provided in subsection (e). Such fees shall be paid to the state
7 secretary at the time the certificate of organization or the annual report is filed.
8 (e) The fee for the filing of the certificate of organization required by subsection (a) for a
9 limited liability company with 6 employees or fewer shall be two hundred and fifty dollars. The
10 fee for the filing of the annual report required by subsection (c) for a limited liability company
1 of 2
11 with 6 employees or fewer shall be two hundred and fifty dollars. Such fees shall be paid to the
12 state secretary at the time the annual report is filed.
