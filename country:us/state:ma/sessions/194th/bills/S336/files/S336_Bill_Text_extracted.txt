SENATE DOCKET, NO. 1516 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 336
By Mr. Cyr, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 336) of Julian Cyr and James B.
Eldridge for legislation relative to LGBTQ+ inclusive curriculum. Education.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 259 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to LGBTQ+ inclusive curriculum.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 The third paragraph of section 1D of chapter 69 of the General Laws, as appearing in the
2 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after the second sentence the following
3 sentence:- The standards shall include sufficient instruction on the histories, roles, and
4 contributions of lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, and queer people in the history of this
5 country and this Commonwealth.
