SENATE DOCKET, NO. 2437 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 384
By Mr. Kennedy, a petition (accompanied by resolve, Senate, No. 384) of Edward J. Kennedy
that provisions be made for an investigation and study by a special commission (including
members of the General Court) to establish a commission to examine expanding vocational and
technical high school access. Education.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
Resolve establishing a commission to examine expanding vocational and technical high school
access.
1 Resolved, that a special commission is hereby established for the purpose to study and
2 make recommendations regarding expanding access to vocational and technical high school
3 education through increasing the capacity of regional vocational and technical schools.
4 The commission shall consist of the commissioner of the department of secondary and
5 elementary education or his designee, who shall serve as commission chair; 4 members of the
6 senate, 1 of whom shall be the senate chair of the joint committee on education, 1 of whom shall
7 be appointed by the senate president, 1 of whom shall be appointed by the minority leader of the
8 senate, and 1 of whom shall be appointed by the senate chairman of ways and means; 4 members
9 of the house of representatives, 1 of whom shall be the house chair of the joint committee on
10 education, 1 of whom shall be appointed by the speaker of the house of representatives and 1 of
11 whom shall be appointed by the minority leader of the house of representative sand 1 of whom
12 shall be appointed by the house chairman of ways and means; and 6 persons appointed by the
13 governor who shall be superintendents or business managers who represent vocational or
1 of 2
14 technical schools. The commission shall convene its first meeting not later than June 1, 2025,
15 and shall file a report along with any recommendations for legislation not later than November
16 30, 2025, with the clerks of the senate and house, the chairs of the senate and house committees
17 on ways and means, and the chairs of the joint committee on education.
