SENATE DOCKET, NO. 442 FILED ON: 1/13/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 413
By Mr. Montigny, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 413) of Mark C. Montigny for
legislation to save lives through increased fentanyl awareness, aka Andrew's Law. Education.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to save lives through increased fentanyl awareness, aka Andrew's Law.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. This act may be referred to as Andrew’s Law.
2 SECTION 2. Section 96 of chapter 71 of the General Laws, as appearing in 2022
3 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after the word “abuse” in the first instance the
4 following words:- “, including but not limited to the risks of fentanyl and synthetic opioids”.
5 SECTION 3. Said section 96 is hereby further amended by striking the last sentence of
6 the second paragraph and inserting in place thereof the following sentence:-
7 “Guidance and recommendations shall include, at a minimum, educating parents,
8 guardians, school personnel and students on (i) recognizing warning signs of substance abuse;
9 (ii) the risks of using fentanyl and synthetic opioids, including comparing a lethal dose of
10 fentanyl or other synthetic opioid to a lethal dose of other substances; (iii) the mixing or lacing of
11 fentanyl or synthetic opioids with other substances that make detection impossible for users
12 without a test strip; (iv) how to detect if someone is overdosing on fentanyl or another substance;
1 of 2
13 (v) the availability and proper use of naloxone or other opioid antagonists; and (vi) available
14 resources to respond to and address substance abuse.”.
