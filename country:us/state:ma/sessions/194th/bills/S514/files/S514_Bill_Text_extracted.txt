SENATE DOCKET, NO. 1891 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 514
By Mr. Feeney, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 514) of Paul R. Feeney for
legislation to protect the integrity of town meetings. Election Laws.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 417 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to protect the integrity of town meetings.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 32 of chapter 56 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by inserting, in line 3, after the word “vote” the following words:- or to
3 incentivize their attendance or absence at a town meeting.
