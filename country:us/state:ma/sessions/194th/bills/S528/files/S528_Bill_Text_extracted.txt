SENATE DOCKET, NO. 2138 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 528
By Ms. Rausch, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 528) of Rebecca L. Rausch for
legislation to enhance jurisdiction of the state ballot law commission. Election Laws.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act enhancing jurisdiction of the state ballot law commission.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 70E of chapter 53 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out, in line 24, the word “No” and inserting in
3 place thereof the following words:- Except as provided in sections 4 and 5 of chapter 55B, no.
4 SECTION 2. Section 4 of chapter 55B of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
5 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting, in line 4, after the word “ballot” the following
6 words:- , placement of names on a presidential primary ballot.
7 SECTION 3. Said section 4 of chapter 55B, as so appearing, is hereby further amended
8 by inserting, in line 8, after the word “any” the following words:- candidate or.
9 SECTION 4. Said section 4 of chapter 55B, as so appearing, is hereby further amended
10 by striking out, in line 11, the word “election,” and inserting in place thereof the following
11 words:- election; the placement of names on a presidential primary ballot consistent with the
12 provisions of section 70E of chapter 53;.
1 of 2
13 SECTION 5. Said chapter 55B, as so appearing, is hereby further amended in section 5
14 by inserting after the first sentence the following words:- Objections to all other presidential
15 primary candidates whose names are placed on a presidential primary ballot consistent with the
16 provisions of section 70E of chapter 53 shall be filed with the state secretary by five o’clock post
17 meridian on the third business day following the announcement by the secretary of the order of
18 names to be printed on the presidential primary ballot.
19 SECTION 6. Said section 5 of chapter 55B, as so appearing, is hereby further amended
20 by inserting, in line 29, before the word “state” the following words:- presidential or.
21 SECTION 7. Said section 5 of chapter 55B, as so appearing, is hereby further amended
22 by inserting, in line 32, before the word “nomination” the following words:- the placement of
23 names on a presidential primary ballot consistent with the provisions of section 70E of chapter
24 53 or.
2 of 2
