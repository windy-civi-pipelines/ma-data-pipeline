SENATE DOCKET, NO. 430 FILED ON: 1/13/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 581
By Mr. Durant, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 581) of Peter J. Durant for
legislation relative to the Underground Storage Tank Petroleum Cleanup Fund Administrative
Review Board. Environment and Natural Resources.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 504 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to the Underground Storage Tank Petroleum Cleanup Fund Administrative
Review Board.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 8 of Chapter 21J of the Massachusetts General Laws, as appearing
2 in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out the words “Massachusetts
3 Petroleum Council” and inserting in place thereof the words “American Petroleum Institute or its
4 successor”.
5 SECTION 2. Said section is further amended by inserting after the words “New England
6 Convenience Store and Energy Marketers Association, Inc.” the words “or its successor”.
7 SECTION 3. Said section is further amended by inserting after the words “New England
8 Service Station and Automotive Repair Association” the words “or its successor”.
