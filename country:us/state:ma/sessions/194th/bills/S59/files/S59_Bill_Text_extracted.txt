SENATE DOCKET, NO. 634 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 59
By Mr. Fattman (by request), a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 59) of Normand R.
Champigny, and Joseph D. McKenna for legislations relative to the right to farm. Agriculture.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE DOCKET, NO. 3160 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to farming in the town of Sutton.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Pursuant to Article 97 of the Amendments to the Constitution of the
2 Commonwealth of Massachusetts, and in accordance with the declaration of restriction recorded
3 in the Worcester County District Registry of Deeds at plan book 863 as plan 2, land located
4 within lot 9 at West Millbury Road and Eight Lots Road in the town of Sutton, shall be permitted
5 for farm use as defined by section 1A of chapter 128 of the General Laws, as appearing in the
6 2022 Official Edition.
7 SECTION 2. Nothing in this act shall exempt said parcel from farming limitations in
8 section 3 of chapter 40A of the General Laws.
9 SECTION 3. Nothing in this act shall exempt said parcel from farming limitations in
10 section 125A of chapter 111 of the General Laws.
1 of 2
11 SECTION 4. Nothing in this act shall exempt said parcel from farming limitations in
12 section 6 of chapter 243 of the General Laws.
13 SECTION 5. The executive office of energy and environmental affairs, in consultation
14 with the Massachusetts division of fish wildlife, shall establish guidelines to implement this act
15 no later than 90 days following its passage.
16 SECTION 6. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
2 of 2
