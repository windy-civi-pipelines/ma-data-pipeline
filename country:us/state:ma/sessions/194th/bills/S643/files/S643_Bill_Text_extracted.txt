SENATE DOCKET, NO. 740 FILED ON: 1/14/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 643
By Mr. Moore, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 643) of Michael O. Moore for
legislation to define recyclable beverage containers. Environment and Natural Resources.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act defining recyclable beverage containers.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 321 of Chapter 94 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by inserting, after the definition of “Rigid plastic container”, the following
3 new definition:-
4 “Recyclable beverage container”, any beverage container which is composed of a highly
5 recyclable material, including but not limited to aluminum and polyethylene terephthalate, and so
6 constructed and designed that it is capable of being recycled into a new beverage container no
7 less than five times and can be collected through municipal and private collection systems or
8 statewide deposit return systems; recyclable beverage containers shall not be defined as “single-
9 use”.
