SENATE DOCKET, NO. 136 FILED ON: 1/8/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 67
By Mr. Moore, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 67) of Michael O. Moore, Hannah
Kane, Joanne M. Comerford, James B. Eldridge and others for legislation to reimburse the
George L. Darey Inland Fisheries and Game Fund. Agriculture.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to reimburse the George L. Darey Inland Fisheries and Game Fund.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 11 of chapter 131 of the general laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by adding the following sentence to the end of the section:-
3 “The George L. Darey inland fisheries and game fund shall be reimbursed annually from
4 the general fund for all loss of revenue for any fishing or hunting license provided at a discount
5 and for any license provided free of charge.”
