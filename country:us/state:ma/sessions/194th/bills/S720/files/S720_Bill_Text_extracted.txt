SENATE DOCKET, NO. 2538 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 720
By Mr. Cyr, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 720) of Julian Cyr for legislation to
promote climate resilient homes by establishing the Strengthen Massachusetts Homes Program
and Fund. Financial Services.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to promote climate resilient homes.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 175 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after section 230 the
2 following section:-
3 Section 231. (a) The following words, as used in this section, shall have the following
4 meanings: “Program,” the Strengthen Massachusetts Homes Program.
5 “Fund,” the Strengthen Massachusetts Homes Fund.
6 “windstorm,” means any storm with a damaging or destructive wind component.
7 (b) There is established within the division of insurance the Strengthen Massachusetts
8 Homes Program.
9 (c) There is established within the treasury of the commonwealth the Strengthen
10 Massachusetts Homes Fund, for the use of the commissioner of insurance to administer the
11 program.
1 of 3
12 (d) This chapter does not create an entitlement for property owners or obligate the
13 commonwealth in any way to fund the inspection or retrofitting of residential property in the
14 commonwealth. Implementation of this program is subject to annual legislative appropriations,
15 receipt of federal grants or funds, or receipt of other sources of grants or funds. The division
16 shall use its best efforts to obtain grants or funds from the federal government or other funding
17 sources to supplement the financial resources of the program that may be provided by the
18 commonwealth.
19 (e) Monies in the program shall be deposited in the Strengthen Massachusetts Homes
20 Fund. Monies shall not lapse, unless otherwise specified under federal funding or a federal grant,
21 or a grant or funds from another source, or be transferred to the General Fund or other
22 commonwealth funds and shall not be redistributed. Monies shall be used for the purpose of
23 assisting the program in performing all acts that relate to the function and purpose of the
24 program.
25 (f) The program shall apply for financial grants to retrofit property covered by basic
26 property insurance as defined in section 1 of chapter 175C to resist loss due to flood, hurricane,
27 tornado or other windstorm.
28 (g) The program may also make grants or funding available to nonprofit entities for
29 projects to retrofit an insurable property to resist loss due to hurricane, tornado or other
30 catastrophic windstorm events if such grants or funding to nonprofit entities are allowable under
31 grant or funding rules, requirements, guidelines or criteria. However, a nonprofit entity shall
32 agree to administer the grants or funds as the program would be required to administer grants or
2 of 3
33 funds and the entity shall provide documentation to the division in a timely manner as requested
34 by the division of insurance.
35 (h) All mitigation shall be based upon the securing of all required local permits and
36 applicable inspections in keeping with local building codes and other applicable guidance on the
37 fortification of homes to resist loss due to climate change as may be published by the
38 commonwealth office of climate science. Mitigation projects are subject to random reinspection
39 of all projects.
40 (i) The division of insurance may promulgate rules and eligibility requirements necessary
41 for the proper administration of this chapter and pursuant to any instructions or requirements on
42 grants or funds received by the program.
3 of 3
