SENATE DOCKET, NO. 2559 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 865
By Mr. Finegold, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 865) of Barry R. Finegold for
legislation to expand the moral obligation bond program to acute care hospitals. Health Care
Financing.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act expanding the moral obligation bond program to acute care hospitals.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Paragraph (n1/2) of section 5 of chapter 614 of the acts of 1968, as inserted
2 by section 47 of chapter 288 of the Acts of 2010, is hereby amended by striking out the words
3 “community hospitals” and inserting in place thereof the following:- acute care hospitals.
4 SECTION 2. Subsection (g) of section 10 of said chapter 614, as amended by section 21
5 of chapter 9 of the Acts of 2011, is hereby further amended by striking out the words
6 “community hospital” in each instance in which they appear and inserting in place thereof the
7 following:- “acute care hospital”.
8 SECTION 3. Said subsection (g) of said section 10 of said chapter 614, as so appearing,
9 is hereby further amended by striking out the words “community hospitals” in each instance in
10 which they appear and inserting in place thereof the following:- “acute care hospitals”.
11 SECTION 4. Said subsection (g) of said section 10 of said chapter 614, as so appearing,
12 is hereby amended by striking out paragraph (12) in its entirety.
