SENATE DOCKET, NO. 2399 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 93
By Mr. O'Connor, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 93) of Patrick M. O'Connor for
legislation to dedicate one-percent of the recreational marijuana excise to youth substance use
prevention. Cannabis Policy.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE SENATE, NO. 60 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act dedicating one-percent of the recreational marijuana excise tax to youth substance use
prevention.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 4 of chapter 334 of the acts of 2016 is hereby amended, in section
2 2, by inserting the following at the end thereof:
3 One percent of the tax imposed under this section shall be deposited into the Prevention
4 and Wellness Trust Fund for the cost of evidence-based public health programs dedicated to
5 primary prevention of youth substance use.
