SENATE DOCKET, NO. 2646 FILED ON: 2/5/2025
considering the Senate Report relative to proposed Joint Rules of the Senate and House of
Representatives for 2025-2026 (Senate, No. 15).
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
Senate, February 06, 2025.
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
1 Ordered, That notwithstanding Senate Rule 7 or any other rule to the contrary, the Report
2 of the Temporary Committee on Rules recommending proposed Joint Rules to be adopted as
3 permanent rules of the Senate and House of Representatives for 2025-2026 (Senate, No. 15),
4 shall be placed in the Orders of the Day on Wednesday, February 12, 2025. The question will be
5 on acceptance of the report.
6 All amendments shall be filed electronically in the office of the Clerk of the Senate by 5:00 P.M.,
7 on Monday, February 10, 2025. The Clerk shall further specify the procedure and format for
8 filing all amendments, consistent with this order.
