Development

Development
Representative Kip Diggs, Vice Chair, Joint Committee on Labor and Workforce
Development
Michael D. Hurley, Senate Clerk
Steven T. James, House Clerk
FROM: Lisa M. Hemmerle, Director, Department of Economic Research
DATE: February 14, 2025
SUBJECT: February 2025 UNEMPLOYMENT INSURANCE TRUST FUND
INFORMATION
The following information relates to the reporting on the Unemployment Insurance
Trust Fund (“UI Trust Fund”) for the month of January 2025.
As of January 31, 2025, the preliminary Massachusetts UI Trust Fund ending balance
was $1.98 billion.
Year-to-date through January 31, 2025, preliminary employer contributions totaled
$90.89 million.
Year-to-date through January 31, 2025, DUA paid $217.69 million in benefits for regular
UI for 84,977 initial and continuing claims.
This update contains information related only to actual dollars collected or disbursed.
On January 17, 2025, the Commonwealth and the U.S. Department of Labor entered an
agreement that fully resolved any outstanding liability related to pandemic
unemployment insurance overdraws as a result of federal UI programs established
during the COVID-19 pandemic. The impact of this agreement will be reflected in the
projections contained in the Unemployment Insurance Trust Fund Quarterly Outlook
Reports, beginning April 2025. The Commonwealth’s first payment under the agreement
will be due December 1, 2025. Based on current information, the rate schedule is not
anticipated to change based on the quarterly projections as reported in January.
The UI Trust Fund data is also available at Mass.gov/UITrustFund in the form of data
visualizations spanning from January 2024 through January 2025.
