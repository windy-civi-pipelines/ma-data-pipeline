Dear Chair Michlewitz and Chair Rodrigues:
In accordance with Section 2E of Chapter 140 of the Acts of 2024, I am submitting a Fiscal Year
2025 transfer schedule for the STEM Pipeline Fund.
• 1595-7066 (formerly 7066-0035) -- Massachusetts Science, Technology, Engineering,
and Mathematics Grant (Pipeline) Fund, where the transfer provides an operating subsidy
from the General Fund to the Pipeline Fund to increase the number of students who
participate in programs that support careers in fields related to science, technology,
engineering, and mathematics.
Monthly transfer amounts are shown in the attachment.
I will notify you if any changes are made to the schedule. If you have any questions or concerns,
please contact me.
Sincerely,
William McNamara
Comptroller of the Commonwealth
