In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to vehicle inspection stickers.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 20 of chapter 90 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after the figure “$50” in line 128 the following:-
3 “ ; provided, however, that notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary,
4 punishment of any person who operates or permits a motor vehicle owned by him to be operated
5 without a certificate of inspection shall not be classified as a surchargeable incident and shall not
6 result in an increase in an operator's insurance premium by the motor vehicle insurance merit
7 rating board.”
8 SECTION 2. Nothing in this act shall prevent the issuance of a $50 fine for violating the
9 “INSPECTION STICKER NOT DISPLAYED” citable motor vehicle offense as listed in the
10 Table of Citable Motor Vehicle Offenses issued on January 14, 2022, by the registry of motor
11 vehicles and district court department of the trial court.
12 SECTION 3. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
